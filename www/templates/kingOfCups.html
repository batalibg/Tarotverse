KING OF CUPS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Being emotionally strong. Very diplomatic.

2. A very knowledgeable person.

3. A benevolent protector.

4. Having highly developed social skills.

5. A very well liked person.

6. Living an alternative lifestyle.

7. A friend you can count on.

8. Someone who defuses tense situations.

Meanings

The King of Cups represents a warm, honest and generous person who is kind and fair. This is someone who is in control over their own emotions. It communicates maturity in dealing with emotionally charged people and situations. The King of Cups is balanced between the creative and logical sides, in touch with their feminine side, without it ruining their masculinity.

In interpersonal situations the King of Cups denotes being diplomatic, mature and respectful, and getting the respect you deserve in return. You will get respect and trust by giving respect and showing trust. If you show that you believe in people they will want to make you proud. The King of Cups often shows up when you are about to reach your highest potential.

In a love reading the King of Cups can mean falling in love with your best friend. If the querent is single it means they most likely already know their soul mate and they will soon realize who this person is. If in a relationship King of Cups denotes a deep friendship between the couple. Sometimes when King of cups describes a relationship it can mean the couple is living an alternative lifestyle.

In a career reading the King of Cups can mean you are about to be recognised in a big way. King of Cups often signifies careers at the top of the corporate world, possibly even CEO. It also suggests careers as vets and doctors.

Personality Types

The King of Cups personality is a benevolent protector, leader and mentor. They are affectionate and make excellent colleagues, friends and partners (both romantically and in business). This is someone with highly developed emotional and social qualities. They are also very intelligent and confident.

They are great with people and often find themselves in management and leadership positions. These people are very spiritual and have great knowledge of different cultures and alternative lifestyles. They are emphatic and intuitive which they use to succeed in business. They usually trust their instincts.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Cultural intolerance..

2. Attention seeking.

3. Malicious behaviour.

Meanings

When the King of Cups is reversed it indicates someone who is blocked from expressing their feelings, and who is unable to motivate others. This is someone with a selfish streak, often born from a fear of rejection. The bottled up emotions can often be seen through weight gain, perverted behaviours and/or addictions.

There is an evil and merciless streak to the King of Cups reversed. It sometimes shows up in a reading when there is a lack of positive leadership, and intolerance towards other cultures.

Personality Types

Manipulative and jealous, the King of Cups reversed type people want to own others, and harvest love as if it were a commodity. They are very selfish and need to be the center of attention. In relationships they are demanding and controlling. They often turn into being a full-time job for their partners. King of Cups reversed people make it all about them. They are needy and insecure.

They play the guilt game with their partners, family and close friends to get their own way. They always have a dissatisfied look on their face, looking about as miserable as they feel. If they can’t have something, they don’t want anyone else to have it either. When reversed, the King of Cups can signify very childish people, turning their romantic partners into parents.

Symbolism

The grey stone throne in the midst of turbulent sea symbolises emotional strength. The King's fish amulet represents the depths of his knowledge. The fish that jumps out of water on his right side signals his ideas and creativity. The ship on his left side relates his ability to navigate in emotional territory. His yellow and red cape illustrate the balance between his passion and intellect. His blue cloak signifies his wisdom.

Summary

King of Cups expresses achieving at the highest level. This is the card for reaching your ambitions. You are living your true nature through your career and relationships when the King of Cups shows up. You might face other peoples jealousy because you make everything look easy. What they don’t see is all the work you’ve invested in yourself to get to where you are today.
