QUEEN OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A practical person.

2. Love of gardening and the home.

3. The giving of love and support.

4. Fertility and vitality.

5. The best is yet to come.

6. Being a parent.

7. Taking care of children, plants and animals.

Meanings

The Queen of Pentacles communicates a down to earth and practical approach to life and situations. It is important to have work/home balance, in fact if you could work from home you probably would. The Queen of Pentacles takes enjoyment in material pleasures and success.

This is a very nurturing card and taking care of people around you is important, especially by providing shelter, food and a warm place to be. The Queen of Pentacles inspires integrity and warmth, and being an abundant parent, partner and friend.

In a love reading this card speaks of being the rock in your relationship. Your partner might rely on you to stay grounded in a healthy routine. If the querent is single, the Queen of Pentacles recommends finding a partner who appreciates your nurturing abilities. It also signifies a very traditional relationship.

In a career reading the Queen of Pentacles indicates jobs caring for others. This card is also good with business, and can mean you will be running your own business, very likely from home.

Personality Types

The Queen of Pentacles is someone with a gentle nature, both generous and abundant. These people love the home and the garden, and they enjoy spending time cooking, decorating or gardening. They are pragmatic and self-contained. They often do very well in their careers, not because they are overly ambitious, but because they genuinely care about what they do.

They also take care of everyone around them, and put in the effort to make their surroundings look beautiful. People love to visit and be entertained by this personality type. They are the perfect host, a talented gardener and they have a big heart.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Home and work imbalance.

2. Reputation ruined.

3. Toxic environment.

Meanings

If the Queen of Pentacles is reversed it indicates poor taste and a lack of sensitivity to other people’s needs. This is someone who expect everyone to work as hard as they do and make the same practical choices. This card reversed warns of insecurity, hypocritical behaviour or an inability to share with others. The Queen of Pentacles often shows up reversed when the querent has no work/home balance.

Personality Types

The persona of the Queen of Pentacles reversed is someone who is brooding behind your back. They lash out with hurtful remarks, and they always believe in the worst in people. This reversed card indicates someone with a nasty nature. They often lack the ability to be reasoned with.

They can give it, but never take it. As soon as someone criticize them, they feel terribly wounded and victimized, unable to see that they are themselves to blame. They are constantly looking for faults in others, they love the blame game and go out of their way of assassinate other people’s good character.

This is someone who is toxic to their environment. They are often sick people, both emotionally, physically and spiritually. These people have no work-home balance and are constantly complaining , but wouldn’t have it any other way.

Symbolism

The throne is decorated by symbols of material success. The goat’s head on her left side represents fertility, vitality and ceaseless energy. The image of the angel alludes to the queen’s spiritual nature. The trees and flowers implies abundance. The pentacle in her hand exhibits her richness.

The rabbit forecasts the freshness of spring and the promise of wonderful things to come. Her cape is green, connoting her security. Her shirt is white, marking her purity. Her red dress is her love and passion for all things living.

Summary

The Queen of Pentacles speaks to quality of life and material comfort. When this card shows up in your reading you will have the opportunity to thoroughly enjoy your life in a prosperous and safe way. It also suggests having the competence to get things done.

This card has the blessing of being both practical and innovative in a harmonious mix. The Queen of Pentacles shows up in your reading with the message that everything is good and you deserve it. In fact, the best is yet to come.
