DEATH

Upright Reversed Astrology Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Endings and new beginnings.

2. Completing a chapter in your life.

3. Putting the past behind you.

4. Dealing with what scares you.

5. Regeneration after transition.

6. Seeing the truth of a situation.

7. Creating a whole new life for yourself.

Meanings

Death card represents transformation, endings and new beginnings. When Death shows up it is telling you that things will not be the same again. You have outgrown a part of yourself, and it’s time to embrace the new. A transformation is taking place, you are growing and changing with the circumstances you find yourself in.

Death can represent new people, places, work, or belief systems coming your life. Death shows up in a reading to tell you that you can safely let a part of yourself go. Old habits and patterns might have to be shaken off so that you can be reborn again as a new and improved you. You might not even be noticing how much you are changing, and then one day it catches up with you - and you make the necessary steps towards creating a whole new life for yourself.

In a love reading Death often shows up when the querent has lost their identity within the relationship. Maybe they entered into the relationship too soon and are now having second thoughts. If the querent is single the Death card can mean they will soon be in a relationship.

In a career reading Death suggests a career in the military. It also shows up when the querent is about to change their careers.

Personality Types

The Death card denotes someone who is going through great transformation, and has experienced their share of loss and endings. They need to release the past. This is someone who needs to let go of what is beyond their control. They might feel drawn towards everything dark and dangerous, with deep urges to survive, reproduce and have power and control. Sometimes there are issues with money and owing money to others, as well as gaining an inheritance of some kind. There is hidden grief in Death personality types, who are waiting to be unburdened so that a new dawn can emerge. They never forget if someone has hurt them, and are often dreaming of revenge and payback.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1.Abuse and addictions.

2. Blocked grief.

3. Unable to move on.

Meanings

When the Death card is reversed it indicates that something that should have come to an end for some reason persists. For some reason the person is holding on. Blocked grief is often a factor. The querent might be unable to face the pain of loss. They remain in the past, and rebirth is prevented from happening. At its worse this card indicates mockery of someone’s death. There might be a lack of care, support and sympathy for those left grieving. Sometimes this card shows up reversed when the querent has cheated death.

Personality Types

The Death card reversed suggests someone who is dealing with credit problems, such as mounting bills, debts and tax problems. This is someone who might even be heading for bankruptcies. Death reversed speaks of someone who should have entered into a transitional stage and come out the other end. Instead there was no transformation, and therefore no renewal. The person is instead stuck in a limbo state where not much is going on. They need to embrace a new way of life and a new existence. They might wish to change and long for transformation, but are struggling with deep safety and survival issues. This is someone who might be consumed by fears of both living and dying, or might be suicidal. Death reversed indicates someone who is lost in despair or trauma.

Astrology

Death is ruled by Scorpio. Scorpio is ruled by Pluto. Pluto is the planet for transformation, rebirth and regeneration . Pluto’s energies are very intense and can make a deep impact. It can change your whole life, until the current phase ends, after which you might find that you have a whole new set of ideas and goals. Pluto can seem destructive, but when well aspected is aiding transformation necessary for our growth, and helping us to see the truth. When poorly aspected it can denote abuse, addiction, violence or unexpected events we are not capable of handling well.

Symbolism

The corpse in black armor riding a white horse personifies revelation in action. The king, bishop, woman, and child all succumb him, indicating that no one is immune.

Rebirth and renewal of all kinds are therefore suggested. The bishop represents faith in the face of death. The maiden symbolises grief and release of sorrow at times of endings. The rising sun represents new beginning, a new day. The towers in front of the sun are the character gained in the process of rebirth.

The river is the River Styx, the boundary between Earth and the Underworld. The boat signifies the souls that cross over. Death is represented by the number 13, which coincides with the amount of moons in a year, and the cycles of endings and beginnings they bring.

Summary

The Death card shows up when there is an ending occurring in your life. A new opportunity is presenting itself. It can also signify your childhood, and the end of it. Sometimes Death represents a fear, or fearing the inevitable. Death tells there really is no true end, just endings and re-birth.
