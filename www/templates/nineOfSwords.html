NINE OF SWORDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Worry and anxiety.

2. Unable to sleep at night.

3. Overwhelmed by guilt and regrets.

4. Being consumed by negativity.

5. Feeling unworthy.

6. Being overly fearful.

7. Worrying about the future.

8. Living in your head.

9. Being overly sensitive to other people’s opinions.

Meanings

The Nine of Swords expresses worry and anxiety, sleeplessness and being overwhelmed by negative emotions such as guilt and regrets. It is the card for sleepless nights, loud noises, arguments and nightmares. If the Nine of Swords shows up with the Moon, or even worse, the Moon in reverse, then there might be mental health issues.

While surrounded by Pentacles then the cause of worry is usually a financial matter. If the other cards are mainly Swords then this person is living in their head and is repeating a mental pattern of worrying. If there are a lot of court cards in the spread then the cause of worry can often be the pressure felt by the querent to fit in.

In a love reading the Nine of Swords indicates a relationship that has taken away the querent’s peace of mind. If the querent is single Nine of Swords can mean the querent is lonely, and perhaps has regrets about past relationships.

In a career reading it signifies a job that eats away at the querent. The Nine of Swords can also denote a mental health worker or working night shifts.

Personality Types

The Nine of Swords alludes to someone who often ends up being on the receiving end of other people’s cruel behavior. They are sensitive and takes things to heart. They feel unworthy and have a hard time letting things go. They get dragged into disputes, getting worked up and overwhelmed. They crave order and comfort and are unable to cope if their security is threatened. These people often struggle for perfection, which is a pursuit that quickly turns into a nightmare.

They worry unnecessarily, and are playing worst case scenarios over and over again in their minds. They question their past choices and are always stressed. They carry jarring energy within them, and often bring the worst out in others. There is an air of unease about them that everyone picks up on, which pushes everyone to be extra edgy and snappy.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Stuck in the past.

2. Despair.

3. On the wrong path in life.

Meanings

If the Nine of Swords is reversed it indicates that issues and problems have been ignored rather than dealt with, and when they resurface the querent might not know how to deal with them. Instead they try and bury their heads further into the sand. As a result things become very uncomfortable, and there is, at times, despair. The querent has not got the insight into how to deal with their problems.

The Nine of Swords often shows up reversed when the querent is focusing on something that happened in the past, unable to let it go. The querent might be suffering angst and panic attacks as a result. It also shows up reversed when the querent is on the wrong path in life.

Personality Types

The Nine of Swords personality is someone who is so committed to their own viewpoint they are unwilling to bend. This is someone who justifies their bad behaviour by the belief that the world owes them. They have a victim mentality, and believes that everyone is treating them unfairly. There is a lack of self-awareness, and the person is not questioning their own motives.

This person can be very hypocritical and take little if any responsibility. Most of the time they are confused and misinformed, and when they do get all the facts they twist it all around so that they end up just as ignorant as they were before.

Symbolism

The woman sitting up in her bed with her head in her hands represents anxieties and fears. The nine swords symbolize worries and troubles, and what she is holding on to. The symmetrical pattern of the swords hints that the problems are due to way of thinking habits.

The bed is decorated by a carving of a duel between two people, illustrating conflict and competition. The black wall relates a lack of optimism.

Summary

When the Nine of Swords shows up in your reading examine where the stress in your life is coming from. This card often shows up when there is a negative loop of thoughts that keep repeating themselves in your head, desperately needing to be released. These thoughts will create stress and anxiety until dealt with. People who suffer negative feedback loops of thoughts are stuck in an unhealthy stress pattern.

Negative thoughts can result in the person feeling worthless. Feelings of worthlessness can result in health issues, such as a low immune system. Find ways to reduce stress. Look at the other cards to identify where the stress is coming from and how best to deal with it.

Remember: material things can become a source of stress. Detach from the need to have a lot of material possessions. Find a way to have a non-resistance relationship with money. When you do that you will become more aware of the things that actually interest you. When you start living your life with passion. Your soul will come alive within you. When this happens your stress and angst often disappear.
