SHE HE IT

Layout

1. What the querent wants.

2. What the other person wants.

3. What the relationship wants.

4. What the querent "really" wants.

5. What the other person "really" wants.

6. What the relationship "really" wants.

7. What the querent needs.

8. What the other person needs.

9. What the relationship needs.

10. What the querent gets.

11. What the other person gets.

12. What the relationship gets.

Reading

A twelve card Tarot spread that looks at a relationship from the point of view of each partner, as well as the relationship itself.
