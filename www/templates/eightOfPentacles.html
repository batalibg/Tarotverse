EIGHT OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Learning a new skill.

2. Being fully absorbed in a project and task.

3. Concentration. Attention to details.

4. Craftsmanship. Apprenticeship.

5. Deepening the understanding of a subject.

6. Doing the ground work.

7. Creativity used in a practical way.

Meanings

The Eight of Pentacles denotes craftsmanship in regards to work. It can also mean starting an apprenticeship and/or learning a new skill. This skill is something you care deeply about, and you feel absorbed in while working. You are able to work unsupervised, and sometimes you are the supervisor when this card shows up, using your skills to make sure the work is of high quality.

Make sure you don’t get too absorbed in the details that you miss the big picture. Look up once in a while and notice those around you. You are putting in long hours at work, but be sure to make time to have fun as well. The Eight of Pentacles shows up in your readings when the time is ripe to further your career with learning and study.

In a love reading the Eight of Pentacles signifies meeting love while learning a new craft or skill. If the querent is in a relationship it denotes the couple shares a similar creative passion.

In a career reading Eight of Pentacles speaks to apprenticeship and internship. It can also mean the querent is building the groundwork for a company.

Personality Types

The Eight of Pentacles-type person is someone who loves being a student, and developing a craft or skill. They love the initial stages of a project where there is learning, research and growth involved. They are often people who get fixated on tasks, but have great attention to detail and the ability to concentrate.

This personality type sometimes has problems seeing the bigger picture, and they can obsess over what is not working rather than focusing on what is working. This is someone who can easily become a workaholic.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Tedious work.

2. Laziness.

3. Lack of opportunity.

Meanings

The Eight of Pentacles reversed indicates tedious work with little gain. The querent might be overdue for a promotion they deserve, but are not receiving.

They are not being able to use their talents and skills in their jobs. The querent might hate their job, and will not put in any additional effort. The failure might be due to laziness.

Personality Types

The Eight of Pentacles reversed personality type is someone who argues for the sake of arguing, and who lacks people skills. They are often obsessed with themselves and their own efforts and fail to see anyone else’s input.

There are issues with too much resentment and fear of failure. The querent might want to learn something new, but believe they are either too old, stupid or both. There is a fear of change.

Symbolism

The brown bench symbolises practical ability. The town in the background represents that the man has placed himself out of reach to concentrate on his work, reflecting the need for perfection and doing a good job.

Summary

The Eight of Pentacles speaks to the learning of new skills. If you are new in a job you might find that you are gaining momentum fast, and people are depending on you straight from the start.

If you are a student this is a fortunate card, as it denotes being able to put theory into practice with ease. The Eight of Pentacles advises the querent to get involved in their community to learn a new skill that will bring confidence as well as financial rewards.
