RELATIONSHIP CROSS

Layout

1. Querent's position in the relationship.

2. Querent's partner's position in the relationship.

3. Basis for the relationship - the past.

4. What lies between them right now - the present.

5. The future of the relationship.

Reading

A five card spread that looks at an existing relationship and considers the factors involved for both partners. If the querent is in a relationship and is unsure where it is heading then this is a good spread to consider.
