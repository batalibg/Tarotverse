PAGES OF CUPS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A gentle spirit.

2. Sentimental and romantic.

3. A romantic message.

4. An audition. Showing your talents.

5. Being creative and learning a creative skill.

6. Seeing something with new eyes.

7. Loving and sweet, but also shy.

Meanings

The Page of Cups relays a young person with gentle and sensitive nature. This is someone who lives in wonder and can be quite naive at times. There is a highly creative side to this person who loves to make things, sing and write. Since all the Pages also are messengers, the Page of Cups is the messager of love, intuition and creativity. The Page of Cups describes seeing something with new eyes, and experiencing positive surprises.

In a love reading it foretells that romantic news is on the way. If the querent is single they might find out that they have an admirer. In a relationship, the Page of Cups speaks of renewing the relationship through kind words and romantic gestures.

In a career reading the Page of Cups signifies working as a psychic, or using your psychic gifts in your work. It can also mean creative jobs or working with children. The Page of Cups often means putting yourself out there to be judged (such as an audition or interview) to get what you want in career.

Personality Types

The Page of Cups personality is someone sensitive and intuitive. They are very sweet, gentle, warm and loving. They are also shy, often holding something back. They don’t tell you how they really feel, and are good at pretending everything is fine, even when it isn’t. They do their best avoiding pain and conflict.

These sorts of people need to feel loved, and their main intention while interacting with others is to be loved and liked. They are youthful and playful, and they have plenty of hobbies and interests. This is a person who needs to feel special to someone, and they need to be listened to. If not they feel terribly wounded and might lash out in some way. The Page of Cups type people tend to be very emotional.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Avoiding responsibility.

2. Manipulations.

3. Feeling low emotionally.

Meanings

While the Page of Cups is reversed it indicates someone who is hard to motivate. They are feeling sad and bring others down with their gloomy nature. They might promise a lot, but deliver little if any. This is someone who will manipulate to get what they want. They have problems with trust. They need more love than they are able to give. This card sometimes shows up reversed when the querent feels low emotionally. Difficulties communicating feelings are also indicated.

Personality Types

This personality type is someone with erratic and secretive behavior. They are overly emotional and often sexually repressed. They tend to avoid pain at all costs, even to the detriment of their development.

They are very juvenile and childish, taking the victim role so they can avoid responsibility. These people tend to be very manipulative.

Symbolism

The nine cups in an arch evoke abundance and satisfaction. The wooden bench symbolizes being grounded. The man’s red hat hints at his passion. The blue fabric represents health and tranquillity. His yellow shoes suggests that the path he is on is one of happiness, joy and energy.

Summary

The Page of Cups expresses that talents are coming to the surface, especially performing and artistic abilities. Any kind of creative pursuits are recommended. The Page of Cups indicates a time where creative people are influencing you, and you might find that you are more emotionally charged than usual. This card also shows up in your reading when you're putting yourself out there for the world to see so that you can achieve your creative ambitions.
