SUITABILITY

Layout

1. The spiritual compatibility between the querent and their partner.

2. The emotional compatibility between the querent and their partner.

3. The mental compatibility between the querent and their partner.

4. The querent's wants and needs.

5. The querent's partner’s wants and needs.

6. The relationship’s potential for these wants and needs to be met.

7. Differences between the querent and their partner working in favor of them.

8. Differences between the querent and their partner working against them.

9. Can the querent be happy with this person?

10. Can the querent’s partner be happy with the querent?

11. Overall view or recommendation.

Reading

An eleven card spread that looks at the compatibility of the emotional, mental and spiritual aspects of the querent's relationship. It also considers how well the the two of you them can meet each other's wants and needs, and whether happiness for each of them is possible.
