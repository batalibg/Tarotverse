KNIGHT OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Determination and drive.

2. Very charming and lovable.

3. Passionate and hot-tempered.

4. Self-confident.

5. Living life in the fast lane.

6. Love showing off.

7. Impulsiveness.

8. Travel to fun and exciting locations.

Meanings

Knight of Wands entails a person who is travelling through life at a high-speed. This is someone who will be the life of the party. It's almost impossible to ignore this person. Fun and adventurous, life is never dull when the Knight of Wands is around. In situations, this card speaks of impulsiveness, travel, high energy, passion, lust and adventure.

The Knight of Wands often shows up in a reading when the querent wants to achieve big. They want to be able to impress people with their skills and knowledge. This card also shows up when the querent is planning to move to another country.

Tips to the reader: When the Knight of Wands shows up in a reading the querent wants to have it all - the perfect life! Ask your client (or yourself) if they had a magical wand and could create their dreams what would that look like. The querent is shopping for a whole new life, and are often planning to move overseas.

In a love reading the Knight of Wands speaks of a fun relationship, a holiday romance or a travelling couple.

In a career reading this card signifies exciting jobs, working outdoors and work that involves travel.

Personality Types

The Knight of Wands expresses someone carefree and playful. They are impulsive and are usually on the move somewhere. They are very active people who love to travel. This is someone who appreciates their freedom above all else, and they have an optimistic attitude towards life (as long as they are not too tied down).

They only care about how they feel in the moment and never worry too much about the future. These sorts of people are courageous and generous. They love a challenge, especially of the creative variety. They are usually athletic. They are competitive and usually do well in sports. They are very confident people.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Addictions.

2. Arguments.

3. Hazards.

Meanings

When the Knight of Wands is reversed it insinuates that there is a bully who wants to win at any costs. This is someone with acting talent who can turn his charm on and off and manipulate people to get his way. This is someone who is very ill-tempered and revengeful, evil-minded and false. If not a person, this card reversed signals trouble while travelling.

This card also shows up reversed when the querent feels they have lost their freedom, and are being caught in a web they are unable to get out of. The Knight of Wands reversed also indicates hazards on the querent’s path, especially during travels.

Personality Types

This personality type hates to lose. They see enemies in everyone, and they believe attacking is the best defense. They are usually quite childish people. They believe rules are meant to be broken and they rarely learn from their mistakes. The only things they regret is when they get caught and have to answer to authorities about their behavior.

They often have a criminal background and are notorious trouble makers. Many have addictions to drugs and alcohol, and they love to gamble. If they owe money they don’t care whether or not they can pay it back. In relationships they cheat their partners, and are capable of running off with all of their savings.

Symbolism

The orange horse represents determination and drive. The rider’s tunic is decorated by images of salamander, symbolizing adaptability and opportunities. The plumes from the knight’s helmet and at the back of his arm illustrates movement and change of residence.

His yellow tunic refers to joy and excitement. The knight’s armor hints at the querent’s taste for risk, danger and challenges.

Summary

Make sure your passport is up to date as the Knight of Wands promises travel that can take you to new and exciting destinations. If you have contemplated moving this card is a big yes, and a push to get you packing. If you are asking about a career, the Knight of Wands signals a fun job with which you can travel.

The Knight of Wands marks fun and/or action filled work where the hours seem to fly by. Socially, the Knight of Wands indicates someone is competing for your affection. It can also forecast a holiday romance.
