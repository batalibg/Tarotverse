NINE OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Luxury.

2. Security and possessions.

3. Taking a break to enjoy life.

4. Being in beautiful surroundings.

5. Being classy and sophisticated.

6. A lavish holiday.

7. Being smart, wise and clever.

8. Being self-reliant.

Meanings

The Nine of Pentacles denotes luxury and financial security. This card sometimes means being on a lavish holiday. It also signifies being surrounded by beauty, such as a garden, and living in an oasis of comfort. A comfortable home that soothes the body, mind and spirit are the energies of this card. Sometimes the Nine of Pentacles is someone who seems like an overnight success, at least on the surface, but they know was plenty of work put in that people don’t see.

If this card represents the querent than he/she is likely to be self-sufficient and self-confident. If this card is in the advice position, than taking a break from work and instead enjoying beautiful scenery will bring you peace and balance. Enjoy your life and live the life you enjoy. This will give you the energy you need to complete any unfinished jobs and projects. The Nine of Pentacles reminds us to be graceful and tactful when dealing with others, as this is the card for class and style.

Tips for when the Nine of Pentacles turns up in a reading: The energy of this card is someone who has the freedom to be themselves. They are empowered to speak their mind. They can spread their wings if they choose to. Help the querent see how they are empowered in their lives to be who they choose to be.

In a love reading the Nine of Pentacles speaks of a relationship that is privileged. If the querent is single it cautions no rush to settle down.

In a career reading the Nine of Pentacles signifies earning a wage through the use of one’s talent. The querent can also enjoy the luxury of freedom and minimal supervision at work. In many cases it means the querent has money and might not even need to work. If they do work it is in leadership roles that pay well.

Personality Types

Personality-wise, the Nine of Pentacles type people are wise and smart. They make good decisions and are confident and self-reliant. They enjoy spending time alone in their garden and renovating their house to be an even more luxurious haven.

This is someone very content who loves the finer things in life. They know who they are and are not afraid to be themselves.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Loneliness.

2. Clutter.

3. Poor decisions.

Meanings

The Nine of Pentacles reversed relates loneliness and unhappiness. The home is neither secure, nor very comfortable. The querent might feel like a prisoner in their own home.

There is a lack of self-knowledge and there is little consistency. This card often shows up reversed when the querent needs to complete something, but is unable to.

Personality Types

The Eight of Pentacles reversed personality is someone who argues for the sake of arguing and who lacks people skills. They are often obsessed with themselves and their own efforts and fail to see anyone else’s input.

There are issues with too much resentment and fear of failure. The querent might want to learn something new, but believe they are either too old, too stupid or both. There is a fear of change.

Symbolism

The vineyard symbolizes success and accomplishment. The garden represents a place for enjoyment and rest. In her left hand sits a falcon, standing for focus on our desires and our goals, and doing what is necessary to bring our desires into our reality. The manorial house in the background suggests security and possessions. The two trees in the background refer to substance and vitality.

Summary

The Nine of Pentacles expresses the confidence you have once you are applying yourself in the highest and best way. We all have an infinite amount of talents and abilities - the question is this: are we developing them so that we can apply ourselves and make ourselves proud.

The feeling of contentment that comes out of developing your talents is priceless, and it is the highest reward for your efforts. Thus, the Nine of Pentacles denotes success from investing and developing your skills and talents.
