SEVEN CARD HORSESHOE

Layout

1. The past.

2. The present.

3. Hidden influences.

4. Obstacles.

5. Attitudes of others.

6. What should the querent do?.

7. The Outcome.

Reading

A seven card spread that can be used to answer a specific question or give a general view of what's going on and what is likely to happen. It considers the attitudes of others involved in the current situation and what the querent needs to do.
