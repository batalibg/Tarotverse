NINE OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Standing your ground.

2. Finding your courage.

3. Knowing what you believe in.

4. Protecting your family.

5. Finding out you are stronger than you could ever imagine.

6. Learning something new about yourself.

7. Never giving up.

8. Being suspicious of others.

Meanings

The Nine of Wands is the card for strength, courage, resilience and endurance. Never give up and never surrender. Things might not turn out exactly as you wanted, but if you hang in there you will get something much better.

You will also learn something new about yourself that is invaluable. The Nine of Wands often shows up when the querent is in the middle of a rapid learning curve.

In a love reading it marks being in a stressful relationship, or that the querent is protective of their relationship.

In a career reading the Nine of Wands signifies a position in defense, such as careers in customs, security, police or fire brigade.

Personality Types

The Nine of Wands indicates someone with boundless potential to achieve their goals and desires. They are often planning a significant move or are in the process of accomplishing a long-term goal. They are also very supportive of their children’s goals. They are very protective of their home and loved ones.

This is someone who is optimistic about the future and will never give up. They will never surrender. They are patient and resilient, and in the long run they usually get what they want.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Weakness.

2. Carelessness.

3. Obstacles.

Meanings

When the Nine of Wands shows up reversed it indicates weakness and stubbornness rather, than strength and willpower. There is waste of energy or lack of energy to save something. The querent might not care enough about what they have built to save and stand by it, or they are holding their onto their position out of stubbornness, rather than because they really care about what they are defending.

Doubts and fears that should have been dispelled remain. The obstacles might seem too big for the querent to overcome. There is confusion, possibly even injuries to the head.

Personality Types

When reversed, this card bespeaks someone with unorthodox views who often ends up either alienating others or becoming alienated themselves. They need to learn to get along with others, and be more supportive of themselves and their children. They often give up before they try, and they don’t believe in themselves.

The Nine of Wands reversed also signals someone who believes they have nothing left to lose, and that they are ready to walk away from whatever is going on in their lives. They have given up on defending their territory as they didn’t truly want it in the first place.

Symbolism

The nine Wands evinces the beliefs and values that need to be protected by the querent. The man holding the wand is injured and ready to fight, inferring strength and determination. The mountains in the background symbolize boundaries.

His orange tunic connotes domination and action. The man is standing on a yellow stage, representing being on an intellectual high ground.

Summary

The Nine of Wands means protecting what your care most about. You are heavily invested in your life right now, and you know how to handle almost any situation. This is the card for people who work behind the scenes to make it easy for everyone else.

It might not be the easiest of jobs, and you have come too far to throw it all away. No one can do the job as well as you. The Nine of Wands speaks to the roles we were born to do. Sometimes the only reward is that you know you are making a difference.
