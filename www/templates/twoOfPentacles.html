TWO OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Juggling finances and demands.

2. Working two jobs. The ups and downs of life.

3. Playing with money.

4. Doing something you enjoy. Turning a hobby into a career.

5. Having fun at work.

6. Winning even if you haven't put much effort in.

7. Working on many projects to avoid boredom.

Meanings

The Two of Pentacles insinuates juggling with finances. You might have income from more than one source. It also forecasts investing money into different projects, or bartering. Sometimes this card means you are stretching your money to reach as far as possible, or that you are playing with money. The Two of Pentacles also means balancing your finances, and make financial priorities/choices.

The Two of Pentacles has a non-resistant energy, and suggests a successful move or change in the realm of money. You might find yourself winning even if you haven't put the effort in. It often shows up when the querent is having fun at work, or is in the process of turning a hobby into a career.

In a love reading the Two of Pentacles represents the ups and downs of a relationship. This relationship might go in circles rather than moving forward. If the querent is single it can flag that the querent is too stuck in their way to adapt to a relationship. The Two of Pentacles can mean the querent has been single for a while, and might remain single unless they change their habits.

In a career reading this card speaks of turning a hobby into a career. The querent might be partly self-employed. The Two of Pentacles also speaks to careers in linguistics or literacy.

Personality Types

The Two of Pentacles expresses someone who seems to always be playing rather than working. They make everything look so easy and effortless, and they succeed even when they don't put much effort in. These types are always busy doing something, and they are masters at making work a fun experience.

They are always going with the flow in life and can do well with very little money, as well as managing great wealth. These people often have several sources of income, and work towards turning a hobby into a career.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Disorganized.

2. Losing important documents.

3. Outgrowing your job.

Meanings

The Two of Pentacles reversed suggests that the querent is unable to juggle the demands of life. They lose sight of finances and the practical aspects of life. There might be chaos rather than security. The querent’s carefree nature might be more forced than natural when this card shows up reversed.

This card often shows up reversed when the querent has out-grown a career or work path and must look at something new. There might also be emotional instability causing problems.

Personality Types

When the Two of Pentacles is reversed it points to someone who has problems taking responsibility for their life, and wants others to make decisions for them. They can be selfish and play games to further their own interests.

Under the surface they are dealing with deep-seated worthiness issues, and are craving attention and validation from others to feel good about themselves.

Symbolism

The two pentacles are money and security coming in from several sources. They form an infinity symbol which refers to infinite possibilities. The man is juggling, exhibiting that he is playing with money.

The two ships are riding the waves, which represents financial ups and downs being easily managed. His high hat symbolizes capitalism and also his ability to dig deep to find solutions. His orange clothes stand for passion, skill and energy.

Summary

The Two of Pentacles often denotes someone turning a hobby into a career. They are still working in their ‘day jobs’ and are investing time, money and efforts to realize their dreams. The Two of Pentacles is also a warning to be patient.

Prepare yourself for greatness; practice and educate yourself to improve your prospects. When the time is right you will step up and claim your price.
