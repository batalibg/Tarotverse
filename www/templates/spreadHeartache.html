HEARTACHE

Layout

1. Did the querent play a part in creating the problem?

2. Does the querent acknowledge their part in the problem?

3. Did the querent's partner play a part in creating the problem?

4. Does the querent's partner acknowledge their part in the problem?

5. Is somebody else instrumental in creating this problem?

6. Other influences affecting the situation or creating the problem.

7. What the querent can do to help the situation.

8. What the querent's partner can do to help the situation.

9. Can the relationship be saved?

10. Is the relationship worth saving?

Reading

A ten card spread that looks at the problems causing heartache within a relationship. It considers what needs to be done, whether the relationship will survive, and whether it is worth saving.
