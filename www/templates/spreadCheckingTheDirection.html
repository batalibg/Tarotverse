CHECKING THE DIRECTION

Layout

1. The power of the past.

2. The power of the present.

3. The power of the future.

4. Suggestions and tips for the querent #1.

5. Suggestions and tips for the querent #2.

6. Obstacles that the querent must overcome or avoid.

7. The outcome.

Reading

A seven card spread for looking at the querent's current trajectory in life, and what trends are shown. Useful for giving hints and tips to the querent about what they can do to help themselves, and what pitfalls to avoid.
