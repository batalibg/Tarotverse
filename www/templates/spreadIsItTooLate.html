IS IT TOO LATE?

Layout

1. The querent's current position.

2. The querent's ex-partner's or partner's current position.

3. Positive actions that should be taken by querent.

4. Negative actions that should not be taken by querent.

5. Other influences that may affect the situation for good or bad.

6. The time scale that a possible reunion/reconciliation may take.

7. The degree of success that may be achieved in the short term.

8. The degree of success that may be achieved in the long term.

9. Overall view and/or recommendation.

Reading

A nine card spread that can be used to look at the possibility of a successful reunion for couples that have parted, as well as the possibility of a successful reconciliation for couples that are still together but may be nearing breaking point.
