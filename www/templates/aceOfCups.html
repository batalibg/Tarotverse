ACE OF CUPS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A new relationship.

2. A creative pursuit.

3. Happiness. Feeling whole.

4. Being connected to your heart and soul.

5. Channeling. Intuition.

6. Seeking the deeper meaning of life.

7. Forgiveness.

Meanings

The Ace of Cups is the tarot card for new love, compassion, joy and creativity. If this card shows up with the Ace of Pentacles it often denotes moving in together. The Ace of Cups speaks of emotional fulfillment. If you are asking about a new job then this card will tell you that the new role is creative. When this card shows up you can trust your intuition. The Ace of Cups can also mean forgiveness.

In a love reading the Ace of Cups tarot card denotes meeting someone who deeply understands you. It may also mean you will forgive someone, or receive forgiveness and have a renewal in relationships.

In a career reading the Ace of Cups signifies work in the beauty industry, tourism or hospitality.

Personality Types

The Ace of Cups denotes a spiritual and empathic person. They follow their intuition and are creative people. They make good decisions due to their instincts. They listen to their inner voice. They believe home is where the heart is and once they find it they rarely turn their back on it.

Ace of Cups people have a depth to them that gives them substance and wisdom beyond their years. They are very romantic and joyful. They have an enormous ability to love, which radiates from them. They often have a special light in their eyes. Many become healers and psychics.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Anger, rage, hate.

2. Resentment.

3. Guilt.

Meanings

The Ace of Cups tarot card reversed indicates blocked creativity and emotions. Access to the subconscious mind and psychic ability is also blocked. The cup is spilled empty which can denote the querent being emotionally drained. The reversed Ace of Cups often shows up when the querent needs to forgive someone.

Personality Types

A reversed Ace of Cups indicates someone that's engulfed in toxic emotions like guilt, resentment and hatred. They are frustrated by their emotions and find it hard to concentrate. They are often so stressed that they have no energy to stay connected to themselves or anyone else.

This is someone drained and tired, and they often get up in the morning upset. They are afraid of the truth and often turn to lies and manipulations. They feel alone and abandoned and wonder why they are here. They appreciate little in their life and are left depressed and angry.

Symbolism

The five streams pouring out of the cup represent the five senses: sight, smell, hearing, taste and touch. The hand that appears from the cloud represents the right hand of divinity. The cloud symbolises movement and mutability.

The sky symbolises the unchangeable: eternity and the Infinite. The dove represents the cosmic nature within everyone. The water represents our temporary human form. The sea symbolises our group consciousness. The lotus flower represents good fortune and strong spirit.

Summary

The Ace of Cups reflects the potential to create something truly remarkable. There is love flowing into your life, and it can arrive in ways you may not have been ready for. Suddenly you have the opportunity to have something wonderful, which can scare a lot of people. Welcome it with open arms. Sometimes we have to put ourselves out there more, and if you have received the Ace of Cups you are ready to receive love, affection and a deeper connection.
