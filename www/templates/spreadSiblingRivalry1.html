SIBLING RIVALRY #1

Layout

1. How child #1 sees child #2.

2. Child #1's part in the problem.

3. Querent's part in the problem in regards to child #1.

4. How child #2 sees child #1.

5. Child #2's part in the problem.

Reading

A nine card spread that looks at sibling rivalry and considers the part that both siblings and the querent play in the problem, and what can be done to help improve the situation.
