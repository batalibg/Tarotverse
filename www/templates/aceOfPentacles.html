ACE OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. New job opportunity.

2. Increased abundance.

3. A new offer on the table.

4. Negotiating pay rise.

5. Feeling secure.

6. Making good financial decisions.

7. Improvement in health and security.

Meanings

The Ace of Pentacles denotes a new beginning in your financial situation and an opportunity to deepen your security. This opportunity can come in a way of a new financial understanding. It can be a job offer, a relationship (especially when surrounded by Cups) or an idea that will bring you prosperity. The Ace of Pentacles brings a wonderful message of manifesting abundance.

It is time to ground yourself in planet Earth. Prosperity is on the cards. This is a good time to start new and establish a secure foundation for yourself. The Ace of Pentacles often shows up when the querent has decided to look after themselves better, both physically and emotionally.

In a love reading Ace of Pentacles speaks of an abundant relationship. This is the kind of relationship that offers the couple security. The couple’s health and well-being increase as a result, and their finances improve. They are more powerful together than what they are apart. If the querent is single Ace of Pentacles denotes that focusing on health and fitness will bring the querent love.

In a career reading the Ace of Pentacles signifies a new job opportunity often with unlimited earning potential. It also speaks of careers in business, real-estate and the health and fitness industry.

Personality Types

The Ace of Pentacles is someone who is good with money, career oriented and innovative. Security and physical happiness are vital to this person. Ace of Pentacles people look after themselves both physically and emotionally. They avoid toxic people and toxic environments at all costs.

They make the most out of changes and challenges, and are constantly moving forward towards a better and more prosperous future. They lend a helping hand towards others in need, and view themselves as fortunate people.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Decreased income.

2. Loss of security.

3. Wasted opportunity.

Meanings

The Ace of Pentacles reversed indicates financial loss and a decrease in security. There might have been anticipation in regards to increased income and wealth which didn’t manifest. The Ace of Pentacles reversed can also indicate greed and lack of generosity. It sometimes shows up reversed when the querent needs to adapt to a more healthy lifestyle.

Personality Types

The Ace of Pentacles reversed is someone whose money slips through their fingers. They seem incapable of creating a solid foundations for themselves and are constantly short of cash. There are also issues with commitment, both career wise and in relationships.

This person consumes in a very unsustainable way. The Ace of Pentacles reversed often indicates a very unhappy and insecure person.It can also indicate that the querent has unhealthy lifestyle habits.

Symbolism

The hand emerging from the cloud symbolises something being offered, often from what appears to be out of nowhere. The cloud signifies a fresh opportunity. The Pentacle in the hand indicates that the opportunity is about wealth, abundance and security.

The rich garden represents health, well-being, security and prosperity. The pathway is straight which symbolises few obstacles in moving forward. It leads to an arch of flowers and plants suggesting balance and harmony. The mountain in the distance means more learning and growth in the future if we follow our path of abundance.

Summary

The Ace of Pentacles denotes a time of increased abundance and opportunity to deepen your security in a sustainable way. If you have business ideas or are contemplating an enterprise this card is a very good omen indeed. The Ace of Pentacles speaks to infinite abundance.

Don’t be afraid to aim big. The Universe wants you to achieve. When you are on your path and you are doing what you were born to do abundance always follows. The Ace of Pentacles signifies an opportunity to create long lasting wealth through good investments.
