TEMPERANCE

Upright Reversed Astrology Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Balance, harmony and equilibrium.

2. Mixing different situations to create a better one.

3. People and situations complementing each other.

4. Finding the middle ground.

5. Enjoying well-being.

6. Healing.

7. Getting your life together.

8. Testing the waters.

9. Communicating with higher powers.

Meanings

The Temperance card represents balanced interaction between the elements to create something new and fresh. It includes all the elements in such a way that it brings out the best of each substance. When this card shows up in your life there is great balance and strength between the different areas and people in your life that are working together. The outcome is beneficial for everyone involved. Temperance appears in your life to point out to you what is giving your life meaning and purpose. This card also says that you will achieve the best results by being moderate and humble. Temperance is also linked to the angelic realm, so listen to your inner guidance as this is the messages from your guardian angel. Temperance assists with staying connected and balanced. It is a card for good timing and the ability to adapt to new situations, as well as working well with others.

In a love reading Temperance signifies a relationship between two opposites who complete each other. If the querent is single Temperance means they will meet someone different who will bring out the best in them. They will fall in love without losing their heads.

In a career reading Temperance signifies Humanitarian careers.

Personality Types

The Temperance personality type is someone who is accustomed to compromise, and who has a patient and balanced approach. This is someone who might have healing or psychic abilities. They often combine two totally different aspects of a situation to create something new and fresh. They are great at management, especially when it comes to managing people. This is someone who gets the best out of people. They believe in moderation and are aiming at long-term goals, higher education and far away places. This is someone who lives their adult life quite different from how they grew up, often even in a new country with a different culture. They live by the axiom that a marathon lasts longer than a sprint, and pursue their goals and dreams in the same manner. Everything happens when it’s meant to, there is no need to rush it. Temperance type people are also excellent communicators and mediators.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Exhaustion.

2. Recklessness.

3. Intolerance.

Meanings

If Temperance is reversed it exhibits that the balance between two opposing elements to create a third is going terribly wrong. This card often shows up when two different people who could bring out the best in each other instead bring out the worst. There is no middle ground and at least one is left feeling exhausted and drained. Elements refuse to mix. Merging of groups and families do not work. Creating a new business by the merging of two smaller companies will fail. The environment is toxic and circumstances make creating difficult. There is a lack of substance to make the dream a reality. On a more personal level, there is a hot temper or impatience, and an unwillingness to unify and mix in.

Personality Types

The Temperance card reversed is someone who is going too fast, too far and is taking too many risks. They need to slow down and take a break. Someone or something in their life is driving them crazy, bringing out the worst in them. At the pace they are going they will burn out long before they reach any of their goals. This is someone irritable and angry, who just wants some peace and quiet, however there is little of that around. They are too stubborn to change, and are sticking with what is not working for them. Temperance reversed denotes someone who is ready for change, but is too exhausted to make any decisions that will shake things up. They are very easily manipulated by others.

Astrology

Temperance is ruled by Sagittarius. Sagittarius is ruled by Jupiter. Jupiter is the planet for seeking knowledge through experience, long-distance travel, tolerance, expansion and luck. There is a desire to understand other cultures, and for aiming at long distance goals, as well as destinations. Jupiter is the planet for the querent and when well aspected promises confidence and inner knowledge, intuition, vision, good fortune and optimism. When poorly aspected it exposes fanaticism, recklessness and intemperate behavior gone too far.

Symbolism

The angel is a messenger of the truth, and assists us to see our highest and best path. The cups in the angel’s hands represents the vessels that contains eternal life.

The angel’s feet, one on land and one in water, embody the unification of the external and internal, conscious and subconscious. It also represents testing the waters before jumping into the unknown. The red wings on the angel are the color of blood, which symbolizes life. The sun conceals a crown which stands for a connection to a higher power.

The triangle on the angels dress is an equilateral triangle and refers to spirit, divinity, fire, life, prosperity and harmony. The square outside the triangle suggests protection and stability.
