ACE OF SWORDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. The intellect.

2. Making the right decision.

3. Success and victory.

4. Mental energy. Ability to concentrate.

5. Stimulating environment.

6. Justice and truth.

7. Cutting through confusion. Seeing clearly what is going on.

Meanings

When the Ace of Swords turns up in your cards you are granted the tools to cut down rigid structures holding you back. It is a card representing thoughts and belief systems. It reflects mental energy used to create new beginnings. In a reading it can denote a communications breakthrough, but only if you are brave enough to trust your own better judgement against the advice of others.

It might feel like you have been banging your head against the wall, but if you use the sword correctly it will get rid of the wall and your ideas can go where they are intended. This Sword is also a tool for justice. There is room for your truth, beliefs and opinions - and there are others out there who feel the same way you do, and it would be good to spend more time with like-minded people. The Ace of Swords often shows up when the querent starts to think for themselves.

In a love reading Ace of Swords means the querent has very high ideals when it comes to relationship, and communication is the most important virtue.

In a career reading Ace of Swords signifies intellectual work, legal work and army or police work.

Personality Types

The Ace of Swords indicates someone who is clever and full of ideas. They are innovative and do best with intellectual activities. They use the power of their minds to achieve what they want and need. This is someone who will apply reason and intellect to confront issues occurring in their lives. They have great literacy skill and rarely lose an argument or discussion.

This is someone who is very competitive and will not let go of their position easily. They love taking risks. They become experts in their fields of work. A part of them will always be restless, and they need to be constantly stimulated. Sometimes they get lost in their heads and need to be grounded.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Memory loss.

2. Failure to communicate.

3. Misinformation.

Meanings

When the Ace of Swords is reversed it indicates that ideas are unable to come into being. There might be memory loss or issues with concentration. The querent might find it hard to get any of their points across.

While the Ace of Swords is all about the truth and facts, so the reversed Ace of Swords is about lies and betrayal. There is little justice and no truth is forthcoming. This card sometimes shows up when the querent has to be careful with their words, as their words can be used against them.

Personality Types

The Ace of Swords reversed indicates someone who wants to create a conflict out of nothing. They make claims without having any of the facts to back them up. It indicates someone who is both being mislead and is misleading others. There might be a problem with brainwashing and mind control.

There is no logic, reason, or common sense - just a lot of non-sense originating from this personality type. They are at times vulgar and ostentatious, other times overly introverted and non-compliant.

Symbolism

The upright sword indicates the intellect. The laurel wreath on the tip of the sword signifies victory. On the other side of the sword is a palm frond showing triumph. The hand and the cloud symbolise influence. The crown suggests absolutes.

The six flames is a reference to the Six of Wands, the card for success. The mountain represents the cold landscape of the intellect and absence of emotion.

Summary

The Ace of Swords denotes mental energy and being mentally stimulated. It is a great card if you want to learn a new skill that stimulates your brain power. Ace of Swords brings clarity, and communicates that sometimes we need to spend time with people who share our belief -and value systems.

The Ace of Swords gives you the ability to discern between what you believe in, and what are the beliefs of others. It provides you with strength of character.
