SHOULD YOU SHOULDN'T YOU?

Layout

1. What may happen if the querent does what they're contemplating #1.

2. What may happen if the querent does what they're contemplating #2.

3. What may happen if the querent does what they're contemplating #3..

4. What may happen if the querent maintains the status quo #1.

5. What may happen if the querent maintains the status quo #2.

6. What may happen if the querent maintains the status quo #3.

7. Outcome if the change is made.

8. Outcome if the change is not made.

9. Something the querent needs to know before making a decision.

Reading

A nine card spread that can be used when the querent is facing a decision between making a change or not making a change.
