QUEEN OF CUPS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A highly sensitive and intuitive person.

2. Using your imagination to create.

3. Compassionate and caring.

4. Making everyone at ease.

5. Self-improvement.

6. Being a magnet for romance.

7. People are falling in love with you.

8. Blaming yourself too much.

Meanings

The Queen of Cups denotes someone who is highly intuitive and sensitive. This is someone who is in touch with their emotions, subconscious and the Universe. This card signifies someone compassionate, who cares deeply about the welfare of others. They are a great friend, spouse and parent.

As a natural healer and psychic, a Queen of Cups person gives great advice. They can tune into their surroundings and people like few others. This card denotes using and developing your psychic powers, and creating using your imagination.

In a love reading the Queen of Cups compares you to a magnet, with people being freuently drawn to you. If you are in a relationship it often means you feel appreciated and loved.

In a career reading the Queen of Cups connotes being able to grow and evolve in your workplace, free from unreasonable demands and red tape. It can also suggest a gifted psychic, healer and writer.

Personality Types

The Queen of Cups indicates someone with highly developed psychic abilities. They are wise and kind people who nurture those around them. They have the gift of making everyone at ease and comfortable. They are spiritual, understanding and giving. They care deeply about children and animals. These people are empathic, reflective and full of insights.

They constantly look within and question they can improve themselves. They blame themselves if a relationship fails. If someone they care about is not well, a Queen of Cups person would know without even having to physically see the person.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Dark feelings.

2. Blocked intuition.

3. Insensitive.

Meanings

If reversed, the Queen of Cups indicates someone with blocked psychic abilities with an emotionally unstable nature. This person might be very numb or inhabit dark feelings. Externally this person might be on too many medication, drinking or taking substances.

There might be bi-polar tendencies, depression and/or hormonal imbalances. These people often experience emotional mood swings and the feeling of being out of control. Given a chance, they will disappoint you, and even possibly break your heart deliberately.

Personality Types

The Queen of Cups reversed conveys someone with a twisted belief about love, relationship and family. They might even have emotional problems or maybe a mental illness. Rather than being empathic, they tend to be cruel, deliberately hurting the feelings of others. These people only feel good about themselves when they can put someone else down.

They over-dramatize and tend to lie a lot. They are very unhappy people. They go from elevated and joyful to uncontrollably crying, and and can develop an intense feeling of sadness in a very short amount of time. They can be very scary to be around. If you are not grounded they will take you on an emotional roller-coaster.

Symbolism

The white horse acts as a bearer of heroes. The knight equates to a messenger, often carrying a romantic message. The cloak is decorated with images of fish, reflecting the spirit. The wings on the helmet and feet are a symbol of freedom of expression. The river stands for the flow of emotions.
