THREE OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Team work.

2. Expertise and perfected work.

3. Job satisfaction.

4. Taking pride in your work.

5. Being competent.

6. Practical solutions.

7. Great skill and talents.

8. The ability to deliver.

Meanings

The Three of Pentacles expresses your work, especially where team work or listening to clients/customers is concerned. This is a positive card which messages job satisfaction and taking pride in your work. Look at the big picture, and how your work can benefit others. What is the purpose of your work? This card has the energy of being a specialist or expert at something of use for others.

This card can also indicate creative work, especially if you are an artist with a special skill or if you are developing a skill in which you will become an expert. There might be hard work when this card is showing up, and you might feel the strain, but if you stay committed and focused, you will succeed. Don't give up on your ideas just because others don't agree with them. However you might have to make a compromise with your clients/customers/colleagues.

In a love reading the Three of Pentacles speaks of love found at work. If the querent is already in a relationship it can mean work comes before the relationship. The Three of Pentacles can also mean the couple is working together.

In a a career reading the Three of Pentacles signifies skilled work and enjoying teamwork. It can mean careers as architects, designers or construction managers.

Personality Types

The Three of Pentacles persona is someone who loves their work. They exhibit skill and talent, and are team players. They have solid communication skills, and know how to deliver what people want. These people are often helping others to reach their potential.

These types are prepared to fight for what they believe in, and are good problem solvers. Sometimes they are ahead of their time and must stick with their innovations, even if no one else understands.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Skill shortage.

2. Poor performance.

3. Mediocrity.

Meanings

While the Three of Pentacles is reversed it indicates a lack of quality in work performance. There might be issues with losing track of tasks, or having little attention to detail. The querent might be unable to deliver what the clients/customers want.

Instead of excelling there is mediocrity. There is also a lack of direction. The Three of Pentacles reversed often shows up when the querent feels they are unsupported in their work.

Personality Types

The Three of Pentacles reversed is someone frustrated with their work conditions, and they are often insecure about their own abilities. This is someone who feels alone in work endeavors, and there is a lack of communication skills. There are often issues with anger as well.

Symbolism

The monk is wearing a gray robe conveys balance. His tonsure recalls the the crown of thorns. The monk personifies a higher purpose. The man holding the plans for the building is the master architect. He is wearing a Liripipe, which were worn by medieval academics and represents his knowledge. The man standing on the bench is the apprentice. He is learning from the architect. The bench connotes his practical foundation.

His yellow apron signifies his knowledge. His tunic and boots are violet, standing for the wisdom of his craft. The three of them together marks team work.

Summary

The Three of Pentacles is a lucky omen when it comes to abundance, as it means you are on your way up the ladder in work. Promotions are likely. It also speaks to team work, and skills being put to good use.

We spend so much time working so it is important that we enjoy ourselves. THe Three of Pentacles means competency and pride in the workplace. You feel like you are needed and that your skills are appreciated.
