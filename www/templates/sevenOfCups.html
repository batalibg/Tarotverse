Upright

6. Poor time management.

7. Emotional emptiness.

Meanings

The Seven of Cups is the card for day dreaming, illusions, wishful thinking and fantasies. It usually shows up when you have plenty of choices, and need visions to make them a reality. It might feel better to daydream than to live in reality. The querent might find it difficult to see clearly what is the best option forward, as the Seven of Cups entails that not all options are going to be positive ones. If it appears with many court cards the querent might be influenced by what other people think they should be doing. The other cards in the spread will tell you what is the best way forward. The Seven of Cups can signify that the querent is searching for answers on the internet.

Tips for when the Seven of Cups shows up in a reading: help the querent find ways of improving their time management.

In a love reading the Seven of Cups reveals searching online for love. If the querent is in a relationship its can mean the querent is escaping the reality of the relationship by daydreaming, reading or spending time online. The querent might not feel emotionally fulfilled in the relationship. In a career reading the Seven of Cups signifies internet jobs, and being a ‘jack-of-all-trades’. It can also mean the querent’s work is in the alternative health care industry or in healing careers.

Personality Types

The Seven of Cups portrays someone who is losing themselves in illusions and self-deceptions to escape emotional emptiness. They lose themselves in fantasies to help them cope with a life they find difficult and meaningless. They need to find significance in their life so that is can be truly satisfying. They often have unrealistic expectations and enjoy indulging in excess.

This is someone who procrastinates and has a difficult time making decisions. These people often have unrealistic expectations and don’t know how to deal with painful consequences. They need to watch out for abusive people as they do not know how to defend themselves effectively and can easily become victims of bullying.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Nightmares.

2. Secrets.

3. Substance abuse.

Meanings

While the Seven of Cups is reversed the querent’s imagination is blocked. Daydreaming has turned into thoughts of fears and anxieties. It can forecast a downward spiral of depression, anxiety and possibly even drug or alcohol abuse. We all need time to day-dream and a place for our imagination, however this card reversed intimates that there is no such space.

Personality Types

The Seven of Cups reversed type of person is someone who has a poisonous secret and/or a past that is poisoning them in some way. They are often extremely confused, and they are unable to see or deal with the truth about a situation. They have a hard time accepting reality and often turn down professional help.

They rarely listen to advice. This is someone who has issues with jealousy or dangerous partnerships. Their lives are filled with conflicts and dirty secrets. These people are unable to see other ways of living, and are stuck in imbalanced and unhealthy patterns.

Symbolism

The cloud represents dreams and thoughts that change and transform quickly. The human head alludes to a potential partner to the querent. The shrouded, glowing figure symbolizes the need for illumination and artistic ventures. The snake signals animal wisdom and knowledge. The castle stands for stability. The treasure hoard expresses wealth and abundance.

The laurel wrath conveys victory, honour and status. The dragon is an agent of danger. The figure with his back toward us is dark from the shade, insinuating being in the dark about the choices and direction to make, searching without any real direction. In the modern age, the Seven of Cups can be a symbol for the internet or virtual realities.

Summary

The Seven of Cups embodies the kind of mindlessness you experience as you aimlessly surf the internet without knowing what exactly you are looking for, and at the same time you end up learning about things that are mostly irrelevant to your life. It is nice to switch off from stress, and ever so often you stumble upon something that is valuable to you. In your daydream there are moments of real clarity. It is advised that you write them down or they might disappear with all the other thoughts that enter your dream time. The Seven of Cups also foretells you are about to learn or advance in a healing modality.
