RELATIONSHIP PROBLEMS

Layout

1. What is the problem?

2. What or where is the conflict?

3. Did the querent create the problem?

4. Is the querent refusing to see their part in the problem?

5. Past experiences with querent's partner.

6. Are they abusive or is their potential for abuse to each other?

7. Is someone else involved in their problem?

8. Are financial considerations affecting the relationship?

9. Will this relationship end?

Reading

A specific 9 card spread that looks at the conditions and factors surrounding a relationship that has run into difficulty.
