FOUR OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A wedding or a happy event.

2. Opening up to new possibilities.

3. Property and land.

4. Expanding your living situation.

5. A strong community.

6. A strong foundation.

7. A life in the country.

Meanings

The Four of Wands is the card for harmony and developing more security on a larger scale, often expanding your living situation, and also becoming more connected to a community. This card often shows up when there is a celebration and a wedding coming up, especially connected to the Three of Cups.

Connected to the Nine of Pentacles it can mean going on holiday. The Four of Wands can also mean you are building your own home or buying property.

In a love reading the Four of Wands can be a celebration, such as an engagement party or wedding.

In a career reading the Four of Wands signifies careers in event planning, real estate, property investing or designing.

Personality Types

This is someone who always seem to be in a good mood. They love celebration and bringing people together. They are normally very happy people. Four of Wands-type people love to play, and need to be free from burdens and heavy responsibilities. They make their surroundings look beautiful, especially outdoor areas, and they love to incorporate all the senses into their environment.

They tend to look amazing, often wearing perfume, and their voices are pleasant. This is someone who reminds you about how good life can be. They are fun to be with and are full of smiles and giggles. They have a candid ability to tell you exactly what you need to hear.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Cancelled events.

2. Problems with property.

3. Being unprepared.

Meanings

When the Four of Wands is reversed it means a solid foundation has not been laid, or is not ready. A wedding or ceremony might be postponed or cancelled. There is quicksand underfoot, rather than solid ground. This is often due to being unprepared and/or taking hasty actions.

Personality Types

The Four of Wands indicates someone who focuses on appearance rather, than the substance of how things actually are. This is someone whose life is rattled with conflict and drama. They need to learn to love themselves before they attempt having deep and serious relationships.

They have a tendency to sacrifice themselves for others and have very poor self-esteem. This is someone who has no idea how to value themselves. Some compensate by becoming control freaks and try to dictate how others should behave. They often end up being strongly disliked by their peers.

Summary

Something long-lasting is being celebrated. Long lasting traditions, friendships and relationships are in focus. A happy event has occurred and the querent is feeling how good life can be. The Four of Wands reminds us of the things in life with special value.

We put up walls to protect it and we often worry about having to live without it. The best thing we can do is to simply enjoy it without taking it for granted.
