JUDGEMENT

Upright Reversed Astrology Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Being a good judge of character.

2. Change and transformation that we are ready for.

3. Taking responsibility for your life.

4. Stepping up to the task. Taking a stand.

5. Being the person you were always meant to be.

6. Recognising your true vocation.

7. Confusion.

8. Hearing a call and answering a need.

9. Unburdening yourself.

Meanings

Judgement cards indicate taking responsibility for your actions and your life, being a good judge of character, seeing the truth and knowing what you want. It often shows up when you need to step up and be a leader, speak the truth and be more assertive. This card also shows up around endings and new beginnings.

It differs from the Death card in the sense that with Death you might feel that you have little choices, and the transformation is happening against your will. The Judgement card signifies changes that you have manifested more consciously by stepping up and becoming more of the person you were always meant to be.

Tips for when Judgement shows up in your reading: it often shows up in a reading when the querent has made an important decision. The Judgement card is telling the querent to trust in their decisions and to have faith in their abilities.

In a love reading Judgement describes a relationship that nearly ended, only to be revived in the last minute. Judgement can mean that the couple needs to appreciate each other more. If the querent is single Judgement speaks to finding love through fulfilling your calling in life. Someone will fall in love with your leadership qualities.

In a career reading the Judgement card signifies careers in recruitment, politics and management.

Personality Types

A Judgement type personality is someone who is very assertive, and who enjoys being the one in charge. They know what they want and how to get it, and they are excellent judges of character. If something is out of their control they feel quite uneasy about it. Judgement personalities are concerned with the long-term future of human existence.

They are great leaders and full of courage. Judgement-type people are quite good at getting what they want, even if some manipulation is needed. Deep down they have a calling, but they are not always listening to it. As soon as they accept their life's purpose, they blossom into someone with great clarity. They often start a completely new direction later in life. Their challenge is to break free from the recurring patterns and behaviours holding them back.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. A burden.

2. Poor judgement.

3. Repeating the same mistake.

Meanings

A Judgement card reversed indicates taking on burdens, digging oneself deeper into an old life, being haunted by the past, and being unable to let go. There is something blocking the querent from renewing themselves. There seems to be no escape from the mistakes of the past and little hope for a better future.

The querent’s judgement of characters is often incorrect when this card shows up reversed. They should be calling the shots in their own lives, but are instead being controlled and suppressed by outside influences. If the Judgement card is showing up reversed when asking about a house or a property it can indicate that the place has ill-fortune.

Personality Types

When Judgement is reversed it can signal someone operating on a negative vibration. They cannot be trusted and have issues with trusting others. This is someone who is a poor judge of character, who believes in liars and distrusts the honest people. They often repeat the same patterns in their lives over and over, even if it is hurting them.

A Judgement reversed person is someone who feels a lot like a zombie, who, due to their programming and circumstances, are stuck in an unhealthy situation and often feel life is passing them by. They are tired and worn out, wishing for a new life, but often lack the energy to make it happen.

Astrology

Judgement is ruled by Pluto, the planet for transformation, and its element is fire. Pluto represents the subconscious forces and rules everything hidden in the depth of our minds. It represents endings and new beginnings. The position of Pluto shows where people search for truth and deeper meaning. There might be issues with power struggles and control. It is important to feel we are in control of our lives and that we are living our highest calling in life. When the Judgement card shows up we search for change and transformation, and we are encouraged to accept our deepest need. If we choose to ignore our dark side, destructiveness might soon follow.

Symbolism

The angel is represents the delivering of messages. The trumpet symbolises the afterlife’s triumph over death, denoting endings followed by positive new beginnings. The white banner with the orange cross is a victory banner, expressing a victory over death

A man, a woman and a child are being called from the tomb of ego consciousness. The three people are reflected on the other side of the river, another symbol of the spirit’s victory over death.

The three people are also a sign of the three pillars of the tree of life. The ocean represents the end of the river, showing individuals coming together for a greater good. The mountains represent the structure that surrounds us all.

Summary

Judgement signifies your calling in life. It can mean you are stepping out of the main stream consciousness to live a life more meaningful. Other people might seek you out for guidance so they also can get more out of life. The Judgement card also means being a good judge of character. If reversed, Judgement signifies believing in false idols, and distrusting those who could actually help.
