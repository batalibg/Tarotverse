THE EMPRESS

Upright Reversed Astrology Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Pregnancy and giving birth, being a mother.

2. Creativity and nurturing in the home.

3. Intelligence and leadership.

4. Being beautiful and sensual.

5. Being abundant and healthy.

6. A good marriage.

7. Happiness.

Meanings

The Empress card represents feminine power, a nurturer and a family oriented person - our mother or a mother figure, abundance, femininity, fertility and the love of the home and family. The Empress is very sensual and confident in her own skin. She owns her own beauty. She is creative and nurturing and in touch with her own inner feelings. She loves luxury and beauty. This card ensures you that it is time to act upon your plans. You are attractive and can attract what you want and need. You are endlessly abundant in this moment. This is a very favorable card if you want to have a child, give birth to a business/project, create a home, renovate, attract a partner, romance, friendship and art.

In a love reading the Empress signifies long lasting love and loyalty. In a career reading the Empress denotes careers in the beauty and fashion industry. The Empress also signifies working from home.

Personality Types

The Empress speaks of someone who is warm, sensual, loving, charismatic and beautiful. This is someone who loves deeply, and openly shows their affections towards others. They are very creative people with nurturing instincts. Intelligent and artistic they often do very well in the professional world. Empress type people love artful pursuits, and turn their surroundings into beautiful havens. They care for the environment and everything that is alive. They make excellent parents and are very determined to get things done.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Neglect.

2. Poor health.

3. Abandonment.

Meanings

The Empress reversed alludes to neglect and a lack of attention, where there should be nurturing. It can represent a mother who gives little affection or protection to her child. The child can also stand for a project, a relationship, an enterprise, the home or a business that need attention, but is instead being left unattended.

There might even be the abandonment of a child, the family or the home when this card shows up reversed. The Empress' motherly and nurturing instincts are gone, even destroyed or actively prevented. This person might even be lost in promiscuity and seduction, with a lack of loveor care. This card often shows up reversed when the querent is feeling ugly and unattractive.

Personality Types

The Empress reversed expresses someone promiscuous, who feels alone and is often a worrywart. This is someone who is desperate for love, and often goes looking for it in all the wrong places. Rather than making everyone feel comfortable, the Empress reversed is needy and high maintenance. This card reversed can also indicate someone with ill-health due to emotional issues.

Astrology

Empress is connected to Venus, the planet that rules our heart, affection, women, love, nurturing, desire, pleasure, feminine sexuality, relationships, beauty, fashion and art. Venus represents the fullness of womanhood and the positive influences of the women in our life. Venus is non-aggressive, leaving conflict to her cosmic lover Mars. Venus rules both Taurus, the sign for the Hierophant and libra, the sign for Justice. Both signs are associated with affection, sociability, love of beauty. The Taurian nature of Venus is more sensuous and materialistic, while the Libran side is more refined and artistic. Venus is exaggerated in Pisces, which with the vibration of Neptune, the ruler of Pisces, creates a heighten spiritual experiences.

Symbolism

A crown of 12 stars stand for the 12 signs of the Zodiac. The yellow background illustrates her intellect. The Empress’s throne of stars represents the loving nature of the nurturer and the mother.

The wheat and the pomegranates indicate abundance. The pomegranates reveal their red seeds, a symbol of sexuality and fertility. The lush surroundings visualize the Empress' connection to earth and her fertility. The waterfall marks being in the flow of abundance and happiness.

The rod demonstrates her ability to create. Her luxurious cushion suggests a beautiful home. The glyph of Venus on her heart shape shield intimates the Empress sensual nature.

The Empress means beauty, harmony, love, motherhood, parenting, family, nurturing, good health and abundance. The Empress manifests abundance through nurturing and caring for others, showing others love and appreciation, and creating an oasis of comfort and safety. It is a very positive card as it portends the querent’s well-being operating at optimum levels.
