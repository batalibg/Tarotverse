TEN OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Security within the family unit.

2. Retirement.

3. Investments.

4. Passive income.

5. Wealth. Financial security.

6. The Establishment.

7. Trusting that what has worked in the past will work in the future.

Meanings

The Ten of Pentacles denotes the type of wealth you get when you inherit something, which could be your own retirement money, hand me downs, or possessions from someone who has passed away. It can represent a family unit that has a very strong sense of security and safety, and the feeling of being protected within the family unit. This security travels with you wherever go in the world, as the secure foundation is within.

It is the type of security of knowing you always have a place to return to where you are loved and cherished. This is a card of culmination, it is financial security combined with the spiritual understanding that all the money in the world mean nothing compared to the love you have for your family, friends and community.

The Ten of Pentacles has the energy of someone who has worked hard and is now in the position of being able to provide for others, especially family and extended family. If the querent is asking about money, this card says that the money you need is coming and you can enjoy the fruits of your labor. You are provided for.

In a love reading the Ten of Pentacles signals a relationship with strong traditions and family ties. If the querent is single they might find love with a family friend, or they will meet someone with a loving family.

In a career reading the Ten of Pentacles speaks to work in a family business, passive income, work-bonuses or careers in property development.

Personality Types

The Ten of Pentacles personality is someone who is the rock of their family, group of friends or even workplace, and they are grateful for everything they’ve got. They are content on the material plane and their family often means everything to them. These people seem to have it all.

This is someone who makes solid connections with their families and communities. These people have made some great investments in their lives, and when they are reaping the rewards they share with family.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Decreased fortune.

2. Poor investments.

3. Wealth wasted.

Meanings

When the Ten of Pentacles is reversed it bespeaks fortunes wasted or lost, or on a lesser note, delayed. The querent might be ready for retirement, only to find out that they do not have enough money to retire with.

Investments might have been poorly made, and there is little savings. The Ten of Pentacles sometimes shows up reversed when the inheritance money is lost, spent or never existed in the first place.

Personality Types

The Ten of Pentacles personified is someone who wishes for more money and success, or who spends other people’s money without much care. They ride the success of others, and their accomplishments are often the poorest of all their friends and family. They are unable or unwilling to provide for their families and don’t plan for the future at all.

They live from week to week and sometimes rely heavily on welfare to get by. Sometimes they refuse to retire because they don’t want to spend more time with their family. Family becomes a heavy burden rather than a blessing when the Ten of Pentacles shows up reversed.

Symbolism

The aged man represents retirement. His robe is decorated with symbols, all which infer his wisdom and life experience. The couple and the child stands for family. The dogs translate to friendships. The ten pentacles are spread around the picture, showing that there is enough for everyone. The archway signifies blessings and harmony.

Summary

The Ten of Pentacles expresses money to retire with, family money or security within the family unit. The older generations are helping out the younger generations. There is order in the Ten of Pentacles, and everyone knows their role and has a place they are comfortable with. The unit is stronger than its individual parts, and the bond between its members is unbreakable.
