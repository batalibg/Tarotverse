PAGE OF SWORDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Gossip and news.

2. Strong mental abilities.

3. Plenty of knowledge, but little wisdom.

4. Being clever and quick-witted.

5. Spying on others or being spied on.

6. Lies and deception. Unpredictable.

7. Being kept on your toes.

8. Being a natural at something (often something you have done from a young age).

Meanings

The Page of Swords reports a mentally very active and clever person. This is someone who can concentrate over long periods of time and who learns new skills easily, especially mental skills. This is someone who is very talkative and restless, and who can get bored quite easily if not constantly stimulated. This is someone who is also generous with the truth.

In relationship questions the Page of Swords is someone who is in no hurry to settle down. If this card describes a situation then there might be gossip and unnecessary drama going on. In a career question this card denotes excelling.

In a love reading the Page of Swords expresses a unique relationship that refuses to conform to conventions. If the querent is single they might find it hard to settle down. If the querent wants to be in a serious relationship the Page of Swords might mean the querent struggles to meet someone they click with.

In a career question the Page of Swords can suggest that the querent is using a skill they learned from an early age. It can also mean communication jobs, espionage, careers in sales and information gathering.

Personality Types

The Page of Swords signifies a free-thinker who harbours a love for freedom and non-conformity. This is someone who is quite restless, inquisitive and at times idealistic. They need to have their own unique expressiveness and have a tendency to alienate others. These people are different to the point where they are weird and quirky, but in a wondrous way. They have a tendency to be dramatic and are great storytellers. They need to be on the move constantly and are highly independent.

In love a Page of Swords type person needs someone who will accept them completely, as they tend to suffer when they have to conform to fit in with others. This is a student of life who is always learning. They are very unpredictable and they are good at keeping others on their toes.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Lying.

2. Vicious rumors.

3. Stalking.

Meanings

In the occassion that the Page of Swords is reversed it indicates a person who lies, gossips and spreads rumors. It can denote someone who says inappropriate things, or who criticizes others in an unfair and mean way. They don’t often keep secrets or promises.

This card also shows up reversed when there is news regarding negative legal matters. On an even more sinister note, this card can show up reversed when someone is being stalked and spied on.

Personality Types

This tyoe of person is someone looking for purpose, and feels lost without it. They have a hard time fitting in and are upset by the fact that they often get ignored, or viewed as insignificant. They long for someone to swoop them off their feet and take them away from all their problems.

They have a tendency to exaggerate their own significance and are drawn to extreme anti-social behaviour and dangerous social groups. Their political views are often extreme. They have to realise they are not victims. They need to learn to have more compassion.

Symbolism

The young man standing on rugged terrain infers being willing to go where no one has gone before. The wind in the trees suggests gossip and news. The orange shoes hints at passion.

The sword held up to the sky represents mental energy. The yellow clothing symbolizes the intellect. The birds above the young man’s head communicate imagination and a love of freedom.

Summary

The Page of Swords possesses talent, dexterity, and mental alertness. It can signify someone who has a skill they have been born with, or have done for a long time. It is in their blood and they make it look so easy.

If the Page of Swords shows up in your reading you are encouraged to use your talents to inspire the world. It is time to further develop your natural abilities.
