MOVING HOUSE

Layout

1. Is this a good house?

2. Is the neighborhood a good one?

3. Will the querent and their family be happy there?

4. Positive aspects of moving to this house?

5. Negative aspects of moving to this house?

6. Overall recommendation.

Reading

A six card spread that comes into play when the querent has found a house (or houses) they are considering moving into. The spread can be repeated for each individual possibility and help give an overview of the suitability of each house.
