MONEY

Layout

1. The querent's current financial foundation.

2. Will money be coming into the querent's life very soon?

3. Money opportunities the querent may wish to consider more closely.

4. Who or what project will bring in more money?

5. Outcome.

Reading

This is a five card Tarot spread that looks at the issues in the querent's current financial situation and whether or not money will be coming into their life in the near future.
