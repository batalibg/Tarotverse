CHAKRA BALANCE

Layout

1. Root chakra.

2. Spleen chakra.

3. Solar plexus chakra.

4. Heart chakra.

5. Throat chakra.

6. Third-eye chakra.

7. Crown chakra.

Reading

A seven card spread which examines the querent's individual chakras and whether they are balanced, or need to be weakened or strengthened to achieve balance.

Insights

Each card should be interpreted in the context of being balanced, weak, or strong.
