LETTING GO

Layout

1. The querent's current position.

2. Why it is necessary for the querent to let go and move on.

3. How will the querent fare in the short-term (0-3 months)?

4. How will the querent fare in the longer-term (4-12 months)?

5. When will a new love appear in the querent's life?

6. What the querent needs to do to be ready for this new love.

Reading

A six card spread that looks at an unsatisfactory relationship and why it would be better for the querent to let go and move on.
