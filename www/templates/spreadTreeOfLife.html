TREE OF LIFE

Layout

1. Spiritual.

2. Responsibilities.

3. Difficulties.

4. Helpful matters.

5. Opposing matters.

6. Achievements.

7. Emotional relationships.

8. Communication and career.

9. Unconscious foundation.

10. Home and family.

Reading

A ten card spread which examines all of the major factors surrounding the querent's present situation. It looks at the spiritual, emotional, professional, home and family circumstances as well as the querent's responsibilities. It also considers obstacles and sources for help.
