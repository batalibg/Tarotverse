NINE OF CUPS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Abundance and satisfaction.

2. Getting what you want.

3. Feeling proud of yourself.

4. A wish coming true.

5. Living in luxury. Enjoying your life.

6. Having meaning and purpose.

7. Being on the path you desire.

8. Loosing yourself in the moment.

Meanings

The Nine of Cups is the card for getting what you want or having a wish come true. It speaks of abundance and satisfaction, and can also be about being proud of what you have achieved. The dissatisfaction in the material world shown by the Eight of Cups is now gone, as this card relishes in all the good things life has to offer; the foods, the drinks and all its comfort and pleasure.

There is real enjoyment in the material world, because the querent fulfilled themselves spiritually in the Eight of Cups, and has returned to the material world with a new sense of meaning and purpose.

In a love reading the Nine of Cups speaks of getting what you want, and feethe ling good about yourself. If the querent is single it can mean they are happily so. In a relationship Nine of Cups signifies love that is deeply satisfying to the querent.

In a career reading Nine of Cups can speak of work the querent is immensely proud of.

Personality Types

The Nine of Cups reflects someone who is thoroughly enjoying their life. They love to celebrate and indulge, and they enjoy a life of excess. This is someone who has high self-esteem. They seem to always get what they want. They are quite worry-free people and find their life to be a magical and highly satisfying experience. They are very content and sensual people. These people believe themselves to be sexy and they think they bring the sexy out in others, which makes them flamboyant.

This is someone with all their ducks in a row and the best thing is that they earned it all by putting the effort in and investing in themselves. They like their life to look good, but they don’t stop there - it has to actually be good as well. They can get carried away in the moment and lose themselves in life’s pleasures. They know it is little things in life that give the most satisfaction.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Poor lifestyle choices.

2. Over indulgence.

3. Foolish generosity.

Meanings

The Nine of Cups reversed indicates that the querent is not going to get what they wish for. Family and friendships might be falling apart. There is disintegration and separation when this card is reversed, often due to lifestyle choices. Over-indulgence in food, alcohol and drugs are also common. Abundance is ruined by foolish generosity and wasting/gambling. Happiness has been spilled out of the Cups. Pleasure has turned to pain. The Cups must be filled with what will make the querent truly happy.

Personality Types

The Nine of Cups reversed type person is someone who has problems with over-indulging, and is living an empty life where pleasure has turned to pain. This is someone who has unhealthy lifestyle habits. They are often obese and have issues with addictions. They can be hedonistic and easily deceived, at times even exploited, due to their lack of self-esteem. There is hardly any self-love. Nine of Cups reversed people throw themselves into experiences that will distract them from growing character and substance.

Their life might look good, but it certainly doesn’t feel good. This is someone who only has friends because they are lose with their money. They don’t understand what the foundations of real friendships and relationships are built upon. They have hardly any spiritual strength and are unhappy or insecure people, depending on money, materialism and earthly pleasures as their only solitude.

Symbolism

The nine cups in an arch symbolize abundance and satisfaction. The wooden bench equates to being grounded. The man’s red hat reflects his passion. The blue fabric illustrate health and tranquillity. His yellow shoes convey that the path he is on is one of happiness, joy and energy.

Summary

The Nine of Cups bespeaks you are about to get what you want. This is the wish-coming-true card. It denotes emotional rewards in relation to something that means a lot to you. There is a true connection to your purpose and/or the people in your life when the Nine of Cups shows up. You might have taken a risk that paid off, or you put in the efforts a little at a time. Whatever the reason is for your happiness, you deserve the happy outcome.
