EIGHT OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A quick turn of events.

2. Having visitors.

3. Going travelling.

4. Being in love.

5. The love of change and travelling.

6. Good fortune ahead.

7. Making your move.

8. Learning something important.

Meanings

The Eight of Wands implies swift action and progress. It often shows up when there is a visitor coming, or when the querent is visiting someone. Travel of all kind is indicated.

In romance this card speaks of anticipation, and being in the early stages of falling in love. The querent might not be getting much sleep and has a loss of appetite due to the feelings of excitement.

In a love reading the Eight of Wands signals falling in love quickly and deeply.

In a career reading it signifies careers in marketing, tourism and aviation.

Personality Types

The Eight of Wands type is someone who focuses on their long time goals and ambitions. They love change, and travel often. There are no dull moments with Eight of Wands people. When they are not busy travelling to far away places or visiting old friends, they are usually studying to achieve academic success. Study and travel are the two main focal points if you are this personality type.

They live life at a fast pace, and are fun to be around, but are not easy to keep up with. You see glimpses of them from time to time, before they disappear into the sunset towards their next adventure. They usually juggle many things at once and expect to see immediate result. Patience is not their strongest virtue.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Alienating others.

2. A losing battle.

3. Being your own worst enemy.

Meanings

When the Eight of Wands is reversed it indicates a lack of energy, of lethargy, and that things are not moving in the right direction. There might be poor time management and frequent delays. News and correspondence gets lost.

There might be disputes and jealousy among couples. Violence, warfare, theft or rapacity are also common when the Eight of Wands shows up reversed.

Personality Types

The Eight of Wands reversed signifies someone who is channeling their energy in the wrong way. They often rush into things and seldom complete things. They are prone to accidents due to being hasty. They have issues with anger and jealousy. They are unpredictable and at times unstable. When they try to do good they end up being annoying. When they decide to surprise their friends and family they end up sabotaging instead.

They need to learn how to be in the flow, rather than being on the path to disaster. They have no patience at all and end up snapping at everyone who is slowing them down. They are obsessed with their future and getting ahead, however they often go in circles repeating the same patterns over and over.

Symbolism

The Eight of Wands is one of the few minor arcana cards that doesn’t have people in (in Rider Waite tarot). This could symbolises the ‘losing of yourself’ in the moment. The wands in the air indicate that what ever you are doing is completely absorbing your attention. The flight of the wands implies movement and travel. The river signifies flow and fertility, while the green and hilly landscape promises growth and opportunities.

Summary

The Eight of Wands can represent music, electricity, wi-fi - anything that travels through distances and allows us to communicate with people far away. It also denotes travelling, often air travel.

It can also mean having so much fun that you lose yourself in the moment and forget about your problems.
