READY FOR LOVE?

Layout

1. Lesson(s) from the distant past.

2. Is there still work to be done or have I learned them?

3. Lesson(s) from the recent past.

4. Is there still work to be done or have I learned them?

5. Lesson(s) in the present.

6. Is there still work to be done or have I learned them?

7. The degree to which I understand the importance of being happy alone.

8. The degree to which I understand the importance of compromise.

9. The degree to which I understand the importance of equality.

10. The degree to which I understand the importance of being a "whole" and not a "half."

11. The degree to which I understand the importance of being open.

12. Is my heart as ready as it should or could be?

13. Is my mind as ready as it should or could be?

14. Is my soul as ready as it should be?

Reading

A fourteen card spread that looks at the various aspects of being ready for love. It is particularly suitable for those who have spent a long time looking and seem to be getting nowhere and are prepared to look at possible shortcomings they have in their approach to finding love and happiness.
