KNIGHT OF SWORDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Moving forward fast.

2. Advancing.

3. Multitasking. Making hasty decisions.

4. Being on the war path. A soldier.

5. Communication and problem solving skills.

6. Being competitive.

7. Being an activist.

8. A truth seeker.

Meanings

Knight of Swords bespeaks a communicative, strong-minded and at times opinionated person who is very action oriented, and thrives on change. This is someone with a competitive streak that doesn’t like to let go of his/hers position in an argument. It can signify a soldier or someone who is in war, battle and/or conflict area.

The Knight of Swords describes hasty decisions and competitive environments. Things will move quickly and you will have to pay attention or you will quickly fall behind. Communication will be at it’s peak, as everyone has something to say.

In a love reading the Knight of Swords indicates finding love when you least expect it. If the querent is in a relationship it marks a relationship that can be of a competitive nature.

In a career reading the Knight of Swords signifies action oriented jobs and careers involving searching for truth. It can also denote a soldier or war correspondent. The Knight of Swords is in the pursuit for truth, and can indicate a hacktivist.

Personality Types

The Knight of Swords hints at someone clever and intelligent. They are talkative, opinionated, ingenious and competitive. They can be quite animated, and they love it when they get all the attention. Knight of Swords type people are able to accomplish many things at once. They need to use their brilliant mind in order to be happy. They often lack diplomacy and have a habit of stepping on people’s toes.

If someone hurts them, they make sure they hurt them back before they move on. They always give as good as they get, and then a little extra to make sure you never cross their boundaries again. This is someone who thinks quick, walks fast and often has a habit of speeding. Everything has to happen now. They tend to be impatient people.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Unable to express yourself.

2. Quarrel.

3. Impatience.

4. A trouble maker.

5. A criminal.

Meanings

The Knight of Swords reversed suggests a person with a speech impediment or learning disabilities. This is someone who is intelligent but for some reason is unable to express themselves. The Knight of Swords also shows up reversed when there is a lack of observational skill or attention to detail.

This is someone who might steal information from others and take credit for their work. It can even be someone who spreads information on the Internet to create fear and havoc. If this card is about travel it can indicate delays, especially with air travel.

Personality Types

The Knight of Swords signals someone who needs to learn to listen to others. They have a habit of thinking it’s all about them. They always try to win an argument and will quarrel so they can argue for the sake of it. They lack boundaries and have little awareness of other people’s space and boundaries.

They have a track record of upsetting others and driving people mad. They push your buttons and test your patience. After spending time with a this type of person you feel exhausted.

Symbolism

The white horse represents the bravery of the rider, as white horses are often the bearer of heroes. The horse is charging ahead, illustrating the mental conviction of the querent. The rider is holding his sword up in the air with his right hand and steering his horse with his left, showing the querent’s ability to multi-task. The sword symbolise his communication and ideas coming through his conscious mind.

The turbulent clouds stands for opposition, and the wind blowing against the rider shows that the knight is following his own beliefs and convictions.

Summary

Truth seeker or trouble maker? That is the question when the Knight of Swords shows up in your reading. This card has the kind of energy that brings about change, however there is often conflict involved.

The Knight of Swords indicates someone who would make great investigators, policemen, detectives, journalists, scientists, activists - any occupation that focuses on finding out the truth. On the flip side they also makes great criminals and troublemakers.
