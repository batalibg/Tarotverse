SPIRITUAL PROGRESS

Layout

1. Where the querent is in their current spiritual cycle.

2. The querent's central lesson for this cycle.

3. Influences in favor of spiritual progress.

4. Influences acting against spiritual progress.

5. What the querent needs to do.

Reading

A five card spread which looks at the querent's current spiritual cycle, and gives clues as to what is preventing further progress, and how the querent can overcome the obstacle(s).
