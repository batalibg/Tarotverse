MONEY PROBLEMS

Layout

1. The root cause of the financial problem.

2. What the querent needs to do.

3. Influences working against the querent.

4. Influences working on behalf of the querent.

5. Who or what can help the querent?

6. Outcome.

Reading

A six card spread that looks at the issues in the querent's current financial situation and what they need to do. It also considers whether help in some form is available.
