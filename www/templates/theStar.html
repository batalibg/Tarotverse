THE STAR

Upright Reversed Astrology Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Quiet after the storm.

2. The future looks bright.

3. Inspiration.

4. Fame.

5. Sudden change and awakening.

6. Being unique.

7. Caring about others.

8. Spiritual relationships.

8. Living your bliss.

Meanings

The Star card stands for hope, a bright future, joy, optimism, guidance, finding satisfying answers and feeling connected to the divine. The Star can signify recovering from illness and/or trauma. Divine light shines through you always, and the Star is here to remind you of that.

The Star guides you to be truly fulfilled in your life. It shines light on your blessings and joy. You might be experiencing quiet after the storm and forgiveness is called for. The Star tells you that you yourself are a star, created to shine and be magnificent, to love and to be loved, and to be your own unique self.

In a love reading the Star denotes having to kiss a few frogs before meeting the right one. If in a relationship the Star describes rebuilding your future together after a tumultuous time.

In a career reading the Star signifies careers in media and in public service.

Personality Types

The Star denotes someone who has gone through a difficult time and has come out the other end with great hope and determination. Star people believe in themselves and in their abilities, they also have plenty of friends, dreams and ideals.

They are humanitarians and revolutionaries, inventors and philosophers. Intellectual, artistic and idealistic by nature, Star people are passionate about social welfare issues. They believe in working towards the greater good of humanity.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Negativity.

2. Being led astray.

3. A bleak future.

Meanings

When the Star is reversed it forecasts that any hope or promise offered is going to be false. There is leading astray instead, of finding ones way. There is a feeling of being lost with no way out. Hope is blocked, and the querent is unable to see any new opportunities.

The querent might be feeling hopeless, pessimistic and possibly even depressed. The Star reversed signifies a difficult phase where the querent might feel no one loves or cares for them. With no guiding light the Star reversed portends a bleak time both spiritually and emotionally. The querent might also be living a lie.

Personality Types

The Star reversed character is someone stuck in negativity while they are longing for something better. They are lost in their aspirations and have become inflexible and possibly even bitter. This is someone who is experiencing the dark night of the soul and who doesn't believe they have any chance of a bright future.

Astrology

The Star is ruled by Aquarius. Aquarius is ruled by Uranus. Uranus rules sudden change, enlightenment and awakening, especially in the areas of public service, conventions and all that unifies humanity. It frees us from our bonds and restrictions. It rules revolutions, philosophies and democracies. If well aspected it denotes ideals, the intellectual world and the arts. On a negative note it brings inflexibility, lofty, peculiar and bitter aspects of a person.

Symbolism

Like Temperance, the woman in the Star is standing with one foot on land and one foot in water, representing the connection between the inner and the outer world. She is washing away the remaining dark matter seen in the Tower card. The bird is a keeper and recorder of knowledge. The eight stars symbolize the chakras.

The stars are eight pointed, inferring that the material manifests itself through the ether, and through intelligence the immaterial manifests. The pouring of water suggests the flow of abundance, healing and refreshment. The water that is poured on land creates 5 lanes, which equates to the 5 senses.

The water poured in the water shows a ripple effect, reminding us of our actions also create ripple effects. The green, lush land evokes fertility and abundance. The woman is naked, which reflects her innocence. Her foot on the ground serves as her practical abilities. Her foot in the water signals her emotional depths.
