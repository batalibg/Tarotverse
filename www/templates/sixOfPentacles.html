SIX OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Giving or receiving charity.

2. Being kind.

3. Showing off.

4. High personal status.

5. Earning a wage.

6. Sharing wealth.

7. Giving of yourself to others.

8. Entertaining others.

Meanings

The Six of Pentacles expresses generosity towards those who have less than you. It can also mean receiving generosity from others. Sometimes this card means earning a wage, or if you are a business owner, paying wages. This is the card for sharing wealth to benefit others and thus creating stability. It is a warm-hearted and kind card.

It signals being in harmony with money and being grateful for what you have. This is also the for people who give a lot of themselves to others. It is the card for entertainment and theater, as well as being a very spiritual card.

In a love reading the Six of Pentacles speaks of relationships that are supportive and kind. The act of helping others is very important to the couple. If the querent is single then they will find love in situations where they are giving of themselves. They might find love when they help out in a charity. If the querent has acting talents then giving of themselves on a stage will bring them love.

In a career reading the Six of Pentacles signifies jobs in the entertainment industry or in charities. It siggests the querent will have financial advantage to help others.

Personality Types

The Six of Pentacles character is someone robust and zestful, with plenty of energy. They care deeply about those around them, and are generally generous people. These people often have a good singing voice and they are strong and reliable. They show their spiritual side through good deeds, patience and generosity.

They love to laugh and are drawn towards entertainments of all kind. These types of people often come into your life when you need a helping hand, and they make supportive friends you can trust. They tend to be loyal people.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Greed.

2. Cruelty.

3. Disloyalty.

Meanings

If the Six of Pentacles occurs reversed it indicates a lack of generosity. There might be greediness rather than the sharing of wealth. Cruelty instead of kindness. Sometimes this card shows up reversed when the querent is short on cash.

Are you spending more than you are earning? Are you borrowing more than you can afford? The Six of Pentacles reversed also shows up when the querent is unable to give of themselves.

Personality Types

The Six of Pentacles reversed indicates someone who has mood swings, is overly emotional, withdrawn and has a tendency to overreact. They are drama queens and can be very manipulative. They are great actors and will fake it until they get their way.

When you see through them they go deeper into their false characters in an attempt to save face. They don’t know how to give to others and are often needy and draining people.

Symbolism

The scale symbolizes fairness and equality. The man holding the scale is standing up while the other men are sitting down, referring to the hierarchy of society. He is wearing green shoes, representing self-respect and well-being.

The six pentacles are organised around the standing man’s upper body as in a doorway, evoking the door to abundance. The man is standing on what looks like a stage, inferring image and status.

Summary

The Six of Pentacles denotes harmony with money. When people are comfortable with who they are and what they have achieved they begin to show generosity and kindness towards others. This card encourages helping others less fortunate. It also indicates earning a living entertaining and uplifting others.
