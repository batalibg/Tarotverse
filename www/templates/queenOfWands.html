QUEEN OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Attractive and fun.

2. Popular and easily liked.

3. Keen interest in magic and the supernatural.

4. Independent.

5. Goal oriented and ambitious.

6. Can create both harmony and havoc easily.

7. Following your true passion.

8. Kind, warm and passionate. Busy and active.

Meanings

The Queen of Wands portrays a warm, kind, and passionate person. This is someone who is goal oriented and determined. The Queen of Wands is also a metaphysic who can use her mind to create both harmony and havoc. She is hot-tempered and mysterious.

This card reports determination towards self-discovery, self-realization and career advancement. There is advancement in career and personal life when the Queen of Wands shows up; this is the card for following your true passions. People will be seeking you out for your advice.

Tips for when this card shows up in a reading: the Queen of Wands loves to learn, especially through stories they can relate to. They have a great imagination that interacts best in creative settings. Help your querents to find ways to express themselves in creative ways that are also educational. They are happiest when they can nurture their creativity.

In a love reading the Queen of Wands denotes having plenty of options and many admirers.

In a career reading it signifies teaching jobs, leadership or entrepreneurship.

Personality Types

The Queen of Wands is someone classy, witty and smart. These people are vibrating with ideas and inspiration. She is an artist at core and has to express herself on a daily basis. If she has no creative outlet she feels like she has no purpose, and she’ll start to panic because the Queen of Wands persona desperately needs to care about something.

They need to belong to a cause more than they need to belong to places. This is someone who’ll make wonderful friends. They love to shower their friends and family with love, understanding and support. At the same time, they don’t give their whole self away to anyone, they keep their inner self safely tucked away for only themselves to enjoy.

You’ll never know exactly what a Queen of Wands type person is thinking. People often try figure them out, as they are often mysterious people.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Jealousy.

2. Sabotage.

3. Revenge.

Meanings

Where the Queen of Wands is reversed there is intimidation and domination. There is a lack of faith or, even worse, faith in black magic or darkness. They has no real friends and no one wants to be associated with her.

There is drama all around, or lying and manipulation. This person might have a personality disorder. Jealousy and infidelity are also present.

Personality Types

This reversed card reveals someone with an evil streak. They use their charm to play people against each other, are compulsive liars, and are often addicted to drama. This is someone with no originality or ideas of their own, and they compensate by being overly loud and animated. They pretend to know about things they actually have no clue about, and are often left looking disengenuous when this is exposed. This is someone with no peace of mind and no substance.

They believe themselves to be very smart and clever, but in reality they are easy to see through. They rely on the kindness of others to survive, and often use people’s kindness against them. They are always working hard towards ruining other people’s happiness. This is someone who has suffered plenty of setback, but they always come back, just like a bad rash.

Symbolism

The back of the queen’s throne is decorated by an image of lions facing each other, representing courage. Her throne is carved in an image of lions facing opposing direction on each side of the queen, illustrating strength and power.

In her left hand she is holding a sunflower, expressing joy. In her right hand she is holding the wand, representing action. The black cat symbolizes magic and sorcery, as well as the independent nature of the querent.

Summary

The Queen of Wands denotes branching out and making people notice you. It often shows up in your reading when you want to have your own enterprise. The Queen of Wands can also signify someone charismatic who inspires you. When the Queen of Wands is upright she is the most lovable creature ever! Sideways or reversed she is quite a different story.

Due to her passionate nature she can be your best friend or a dangerous nemesis. Be careful not to get on her bad side as no one can plot revenge like the Queen of Wands. In career questions she turns up when you need to create something of your own - something you don’t have to share. In love she speaks of someone you are unable to ignore or take your eyes off. When the Queen of Wands is you it means you are attracting admirers.
