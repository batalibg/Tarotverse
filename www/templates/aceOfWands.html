ACE OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A new beginning.

2. Expanding your potential.

3. Expressing yourself in a creative way.

4. Giving it your all.

5. Believing in yourself.

6. Giving birth to a baby, project or idea.

7. Potential for greatness.

8. Moving forward in a happy and healthy way.

Meanings

The Ace of Wands speaks of new beginnings. Be bold and start something new. Follow your inspiration. It speaks to birth of all kinds: birth of enterprises and job opportunities, but also the birth of a baby.

The other cards in the spread will tell you in what area the querent is experiencing this birth. The Ace of Wands also shows up when there is a great potential, but it is up to you to make this potential a reality.

The Ace of Wands in a love readings indicates a passionate relationship.

In a career reading it signifies work with technology and tools. The Ace of Wands also denotes a new and exciting job offer.

Personality Types

The Ace of Wands suggests a very powerful spirit. This is someone who desires success in all areas, and who incorporates creativity and spirituality in all of their pursuits. This is someone who makes things happen, often against all odds. No matter how much is stacked up against them, they persevere, often with amazing results.

They are very thoroughly individualistic. This is someone with a sense of wonder, and they live life with passion. They are full of fresh ideas and don’t hesitate in moving forward.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Immature.

2. Poor timing.

3. Unmotivated.

Meanings

The Ace of Wands reversed indicates that new beginnings are blocked. Ideas and enterprises do not take hold, and there is little if any growth. Joy is clouded and stunted. Sometimes this card shows up reversed when there are endings rather than beginnings. Rebirth is difficult with the reversed Ace of Wands.

Tip to the reader: When a reversed Ace of Wands is showing up in a reading your client might be ‘low’ in vigor. They need to feel more excited about life. Help them find what they are ‘burning’ for. The cards will help you with this, as the client might not yet know what they are seeking.

Personality Types

A reversed Ace of Wands denotes someone who is hard to get motivated. Their creativity turns into hoarding, and there is a mess everywhere. Very little is accomplished - instead more junk is added to the growing mayhem. There is also a lack of originality. This is someone who copies others, and has little faith in the future.

They often yearn to go back to the past, and convince themselves that things used to be so much better in the good old days. They need to learn to think outside the box and start appreciating the little things that life has got to offer. This personality type wants to do everything at once, and will end up accomplishing little or nothing at all instead.

Symbolism

The hand issuing from the cloud and grasping the wand symbolises creation, invention and enterprise at an idea level. The leaves carried away with the wind represent the wind behind your sails and moving forward.

The castle signifies ideas being materialised into something physical. The river symbolises flow, while the trees and fields mean growth and abundance.

Summary

The Ace of Wands indicates a time of opportunity. Seek and you shall find. There is more to life than what you thought or believed. An adventure is about to present itself, and it might be the ride of your lifetime.

Life gives you choices, and the choices presented through the Ace of Wands are that you can either grab the wand or pass it on. Look at the other cards in the spread for advice on what to do. This might be an opportunity you want to grab with both hands.
