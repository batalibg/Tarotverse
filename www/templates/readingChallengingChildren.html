Layout

1. Past influences affecting the querent's child.

2. Present influences affecting the querent's child.

3. Is it better for querent to leave things alone?

4. Is it better for the querent to confront the issue?

5. Is it better for someone else to confront the issue?

6. The outcome of the best option suggested by cards 3, 4 or 5.

Reading

A six card spread that looks at children that are a challenge to their parent(s), and whether or not anything can be done to help improve the situation.
