FOUR OF CUPS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. What goes up must come down.

2. Feeling flat and uninspired.

3. Being self-absorbed.

4. Not enjoying other people’s company.

5. Feeling left out.

6. Contemplation.

7. Being dissatisfied. Being bored.

Meanings

The Four of Cups is the card for contemplation. It often shows up when the querent is dissatisfied about something, and doesn’t fully notice what they are being offered. They are bored with what they’ve got, but don’t do much to change it either. It is the card for apathy. The Four of Cups with the Hermit can denote they have lost interest in the outside world and are instead focusing on meditation. This can also be the case if the Four of Cups is next to the Four of Swords, but to a less extent. The Four of Cups sometimes shows up when someone is needing the querent’s help, but the querent might not realize until it is too late.

In a love reading the Four of Cups denotes the querent is not paying attention to what is on offer when it comes to love. They may not fully appreciate their partner, or if single they don’t see someone who is offering them love.

In a career reading the Four of Cups signifies complacency in work. The querent knows how to do their work well, but doesn’t love it enough to be really good at it. It can also mean work in administration and broadcasting. As with all the Cups, the Four of Cups also can mean work in hospitality and counselling.

Personality Types

the Four of Cups type is someone who is dissatisfied with their current situation. They never seem to get exactly what they want, and end up being in conflict with what they have and what they need. This is someone who lacks the drive and motivation to go ahead and make themselves happy.

When opportunities present themselves, they often miss them do to their lack of focus. They need to open their eyes and see the world as it actually is, not how they believe it to be. This is someone who’s life lacks magic and excitement. They often take things for granted and are often missing the point.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Doing something you will later regret.

2. Emotional emptiness.

3. Lack of imagination.

4. Unhealthy lifestyle and cravings.

Meanings

The Four of Cups reversed signals that the querent has become unbalanced, and has difficulty noticing the opportunities that are presenting themselves. Life has become so emotionally empty that the querent can’t even imagine anything better coming along. Sometimes this card shows up reversed when the querent needs to do more meditation and listen more to their intuition. The Four of Cups reversed can also indicate unhealthy cravings and temptations.

Personality Types

The Four of Cups reversed conveys someone who is irritable, crabby and unstable when under pressure. They are terrified of displeasing those around them, and sacrifice their own happiness for other people’s convenience. There are often unresolved issues with the women in their life. They often suffer from too much feminine influences and are over-reacting to those around them.

The Four of Cups reversed personality type needs to connect with their own hearts, and ask themselves what it is they really want. They need to spend some time alone until they know who they are, as they too often just follow the lead of other people.

Symbolism

The three cups symbolize the outside world of social groups and people coming together. The man sits under the tree with his arms and legs folded, intimating a meditative state of contemplating, as well as being introverted and reserved. He takes little interest in the three cups in front of him. A cup is being presented to him by an outstretched representation of the Ace of Cups; a new opportunity is presenting itself. The man takes no notice of the cup offered to him, symbolising emotional apathy. The tree represents life and growth still being a possibility.

Summary

There is a need for emotional satisfaction that seems to be unavailable when the Four of Cups turns up. You might find yourself chasing good times that seem to elude you. Take this time to get in touch with your inner voice and you will be restored to your natural state of joy.
