YOUNG LOVE

Layout

1. Current position of querent's child.

2. Is this love real or a teenage crush?

3. Will the querent's child get hurt in this relationship?

4. Should the querent talk to their child or just let things take their course?

5. The future of the relationship.

Reading

A five card spread that looks at a relationship the querent's child is in. It considers whether the love is real or temporary, and whether the querent should talk to their child or let matters take their natural course.
