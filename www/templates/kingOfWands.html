KING OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A natural leader.

2. Ambitious and charismatic.

3. Good with technology.

4. A married man.

5. Entrepreneurial skills.

6. New and exciting projects.

7. Making other people feel special.

8. Wanting to have it all.

Meanings

The King of Wands often denotes someone who is married with a family. This is someone who is a natural and charismatic leader. The King of Wands indicates entrepreneurial skills and running new and exciting projects. This card can represent an industry leader and being an expert, especially in media and communication.

This card signifies being passionate about life. King of Wands-type people don’t do well with compromises and neither will you if this card represents you in a reading. In most situations the King of Wands means leadership, commitment, getting recognition, success and doing the things that makes you tick.

In a love reading the King of Wands can mean the querent is falling in love with someone who is committed elsewhere.

In a career reading this card signifies careers in media, technology and small business owners.

Personality Types

The King of Wands is the king of cool, someone very exciting. A powerful risk taker, the King of Wands personality types have plenty of healthy confidence. It is the kind of confidence that makes other people feel confident too. They make great leader,s and people are drawn to their magnetic personalities.

They have a real talent in making others feel special. This is someone very driven and magnetic. They have a genuine concern and consideration for other people’s well-being. They love to invest and they love to push themselves to go further. With so much to experience, they want to have it all.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Terror.

2. A double life.

3. Laziness.

Meanings

The King of Wands reversed indicates dictatorship. This is someone with a superiority complex and a bad temper, often violent. Sometimes this card shows up when someone lives a double life, they are charming to the outside world, but at home they are terrorizing their families with their temper and control issues. If not a person, this card reversed indicates red tape, fear of taking risks and poor leadership.

Personality Types

If the King of Wands is reversed it indicates someone who needs to learn to control their temper. They have too much ego and expect everyone to tip toe around them and cater for their every need. These people don't take responsibility for the state of their relationships and blame everyone else if something goes wrong.

They rarely stay faithful to their partners or friends, and don't conduct themselves with integrity. They are vain and believe themselves to be much better looking than what they are in reality. They also have an annoying habit of believing everyone is secretly in love with them. This is someone who lacks self-awareness.

They only want to talk about topics they are interested in, which usually is themselves. At times they are shameless and promiscuous, and they often have a drinking problem.

They exaggerate their own importance and brag continuously. Some of them have even worse problems and become violently dangerous and unstable.

Symbolism

The king’s cape and throne are decorated with images of the lion and the salamander, symbolizing strength and courage among change and transformation. The salamander biting its own tale represents an Ouroboros. The Ouroboros expresses the ability to re-create yourself.

The throne is made of gold, signaling prestige. The king’s crown is the shape of flames, the reflecting a passion for prestige. The wand in his right hand conveys ambitious ideas. The king’s lion necklace implies courage.

Summary

The King of Wands denotes natural leadership. This card sometimes shows up when you are interacting with authority figures. It is wise to let them help you.

The King of Wands is also the card for achievement. The best thing you can do is to focus on yourself. Compete only with yourself and you will be amazed at how far you will go.
