WHICH LOVE?

Layout

1. Does #1 have the potential to be a "true love" for the querent?

2. Influences acting as an obstacle to a relationship with #1.

3. Influences acting in favor of a relationship with #1.

4. What should the querent do to help make #1 part of their life?

5. Will the querent be happy with #1?

6. Does #2 have the potential to be a "true love" for the querent?

7. Influences acting as an obstacle to a relationship with #2.

8. Influences acting in favor of a relationship with #2.

9. What should the querent do to help make #2 part of their life?

10. Will the querent be happy with #2?

Reading

A ten card spread that looks at two potential lovers.

Insights

The querent should decide beforehand which potential lover is depicted by cards 1 and 6.
