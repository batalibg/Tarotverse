FIVE OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Spending more than you earn.

2. Difficulty paying your bills.

3. Experiencing hard times.

4. Lacking support. Suffering low energy.

5. Being dependent on charity.

6. Lack of confidence and low self-esteem.

7. Being out in the cold.

8. Feeling unloved.

Meanings

The Five of Pentacles signals financial loss and hardship. It can also mean the querent feels that there is no security, and that their health might be suffering. They might feel like they are out in the cold, and that there is a need to receive help and support. The Five of Pentacles can mean poverty and isolation. Look at your relationship with money and whether or not anxiety and ignorance are giving you financial grief.

You might also need to look at your network, and if you should allow help from others. The big question is, are you able to help yourself? If this card represents the querent on a psychological level, there is a lack of confidence and self-esteem. Remember bills only go away if you pay them.

It is time now to deal with your debt before it becomes unmanageable. If you feel your debt already has gotten the better of you, it is best to consult an expert. Now is the time to take the appropriate action to improve your situation. The Five of Pentacles often shows up when the querent feels they have to prove their worth in some way.

In a love reading the Five of Pentacles can suggest that the querent feels unloved and unappreciated by their partner. If the querent is single it can mean they feel unlovable and worthless. The querent has the belief that no one wants them.

In a career reading the Five of Pentacles can signify the querent is relying on welfare. It can also denote working under dull circumstances.

In a work situation the Five of Pentacles can mean the querent feels unsupported in their role. It can sometime signal working for charities and social work.

Personality Types

A Five of Pentacles person is someone who feels like an outsider, and is often drawn towards the bottom end of society. They are often quite unfortunate people with little money. They also experience health issues, and are stuck in a pattern of negative thoughts and beliefs about themselves.

This is someone who feels something vital is missing from their life. They are overwhelmed and unable to stay on top of things. They need help from others to keep themselves afloat. Five of Pentacles type people often overcompensate and self-sabotage.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. No help offered.

2. Poverty.

3. Giving up.

Meanings

The Five of Pentacles reversed indicates disorder and chaos. Not only is there no money, but there are also a lack of spirituality and friendships. People in need are being asked to return to where they came from rather than given a chance to integrate.

The organisations that are meant to help are closing down. The Five of Pentacles reversed often means there are increased levels of poverty and debt in the querent’s life.

Personality Types

When the Five of Pentacles is reversed it indicates someone with few real friends and plenty of antisocial tendencies. They try to buy friends and side with who ever they are with, in hope that they will be accepted.

Deep down they feel lost in self-pity and are afraid of change in case things get even worse. Five of Pentacles people often self-sabotage and don’t really believe they deserve to receive good.

Symbolism

The snow on the ground symbolises being left out in the cold. The snow falling from the sky forecasts few changes in the current circumstances. The black wall illustrates worries. The colorful stained glass window signals that help and assistance is available if you allow it.

The man is wearing a bell, representing ill-health (people with leprosy used to be fitted with a bell). The woman walks barefoot in the snow, expressing having little material wealth.
