KING OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. A business man.

2. Career orientated.

3. Middle management. Project management.

4. A comfortable life.

5. Successful touch.

6. Being an expert in your field.

7. The love of money and success.

8. Being at the top of your game.

Meanings

The King of Pentacles denotes success in business. This is a stable and authoritative character, who is hard-working and dependable. The King of Pentacles achieves success through discipline and is an expert in his field. It is a very ambitious and career oriented card, and if you are being represented by this card than you need to have control and power, as well as security and a stable home.

The King of Pentacles indicates a strong provider, both financially and through giving advice and guidance. There is a lot of support provided by the King of Pentacles, who is willing to hear you you, but he is business oriented at his core, so do not try to con him. He is no fool.

In a love reading the King of Pentacles communicates a secure and committed relationship. The couple is enjoying great wealth and success.

In a career reading it signifies the querent has mastered something vital which provides them with success and recognition. The querent feels secure in their work role and promotions are likely.

Personality Types

The King of Pentacles type person is someone with a great work ethic who is good with both money and people. They are excellent project managers and can handle several projects at once. Several people will have to be hired to replace one King of Pentacles person. They have great observational skills and leadership abilities.

They are not afraid to have a conflict if it means getting the work done properly. This is someone who loves nature, animals and people, who is stable, accomplished and powerful.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Hoarding.

2. Averse to changes.

3. Poor health.

4. Lacking people skills.

Meanings

A King of Pentacles card reversed indicates being too conservative. When reversed it suggests difficulties in making the necessary changes. Falling behind is likely. The King of Pentacles reversed is likely to be a miser and a hoarder.

This is someone who expects the best, but allows others to have the worst. Neglect of hygiene and poor health are indicated when King of Pentacles shows up reversed. Stubbornness and fear prevent positive change.

Personality Types

A King of Pentacles reversed person is someone who lives in clutter, both in his house and in his head. They are often dirty and terribly disorganized. They want to do well, but lack both people skills and organisational skills to get very far. They might even be unemployed or doing unreliable casual employment. They don’t make good providers and never let you know how they really feel.

If they are in leadership positions they often make a real mess of things, because they are unable to handle conflicts and difficult situations. They are very money oriented and dream about winning the lottery. If they do come to money they will store it away only for them to keep, and continue acting as if they are in poverty.

Symbolism

The rooftop garden symbolises comfort and abundance. The king’s throne is decorated with carvings of bulls and vines. The bulls convey mastering the material world. The vines relate excess. The king’s robe is decorated with images of grapevines rich with fruit conveying abundance.

Around him and at his feet are vines and flowers symbolising his success in the physical world. In his right hand he is holding a sceptre representing authority. In his left hand he holds a pentacle indicating financial wealth.

13. King of Pentacles + Four of Wands: A wealthy community.

Summary

The King of Pentacles speak to achieving on the material plane. You are mastering the art of finance and wealth-creating if the King of Pentacles represents you and your life.

The King of Pentacles is a useful card to get in questions about business endeavors. It denotes career advancement and having a successful touch.
