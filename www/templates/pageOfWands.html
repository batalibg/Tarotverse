PAGE OF WANDS

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Passion and determination.

2. Attention seeking individual.

3. Can be a drama queen.

4. Messages of all kind; texts, phone calls, emails.

5. Receiving news.

6. Chasing adventures and good times. Risk taker.

7. Go in a new direction. Do something you have never done before.

Meanings

The Page of Wands portrays an enthusiastic young person who is eager to explore and gain life experience. This card could also be a creative idea in itâ€™s initial stages. The potential of this idea will be decided in the other cards in the spread.

There are a lot of messages, texts, phone calls and emails when this card shows up, as this is the card for giving and receiving news of all kinds. The Page of Wands often shows up when the querent is very excited about a new project.

In a love reading it signifies someone who creates and/or attracts drama in relationships.

In a career reading the Page of Wands serves as an agent of creative work, community projects or working with the youth.

Personality Types

The Page of Wands signifies someone with plenty of passion and enthusiasm. They love taking risks, and are creative and endearing. They often end up being the center of attention. They need to feel like they live life to the fullest and are always going on a new adventure somewhere. Their natural state is that of happiness and joy, however they also need others to feel happy with.

They work hard to pick up the moods of those around them so that everyone can have a good time. This is someone who seeks others endearment. They have a big heart, and they are not afraid to show you how much they love you.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Bad news.

2. Lack of enthusiasm.

3. A bully.

Meanings

When reversed, the Page of Wands indicates a bully or mean tempered person who likes to show off and is demanding attention. The enthusiasm indicated in the Page of Wands upright is blocked when this card shows up reversed.

The Page of Wands reversed also portends bad news on its way. Sometimes the Page of Wands shows up reversed when the querent is involved in a doomed project. There is unhealthy competition going on.

Personality Types

The Page of Wands reversed type people are, for whatever reason, unable to let their light shine. They often find themselves creatively stagnate and exhausted. They feel powerless, and act out in various ways to compensate for the way they feel about themselves.

They often feel like life is passing them by. They are afraid of being forgotten and pursue careers that they hope will give them recognition. They often end up unpopular and alienated, the two things they fear the most.

Symbolism

The feather on his hat illustrates movement. His yellow tunic signals freshness and joy. His yellow shoes espouse happiness. His orange tights purport ambition and drive.

His tunic is decorated with images of salamander. Salamanders stand for opportunities, visions and adaptability. The orange landscape symbolizes the querents passion and determination.

Summary

The Page of Wands is someone who needs to be heard. It can be a child who is constantly trying to get your attention, or it can be a friend who likes to have your full attention.

In a work related question the Page of Wands can forecast a new project or workplace. It is a card that speaks of an overflow of excitement bundled up in a person who has a personality larger than life.
