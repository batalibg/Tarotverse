SEVEN OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Taking care of your Pentacles (possessions).

2. Efforts vs. rewards.

3. Taking stock.

4. Reaching a milestone.

5. Long term investment.

6. Feeling not good enough.

7. Wanting more job satisfaction.

8. Routines at work.

Meanings

The Seven of Pentacles predicts a vision that will pay off in the future. You are planting the seeds now so that you will be able to harvest a more secure and abundant future for yourself. Look at your investments long-term and make a plan for yourself. There is a reward waiting to be reaped if you stick to it. Be patient with yourself. Take a step back and look at the value of your effort.

You might feel you are working hard for little gain, but the Seven of Pentacles looks at the long-term benefit. Sometimes it is a notification that you might not have enough energy to finish what you started.

It gives you a warning not to make any major decisions as you don’t have all the facts (don’t sign any contracts right now). Work smarter instead of harder, because hard work is not always enough, and you might run out of resources if you continue.

In a love reading the Seven of Pentacles communicates a relationship stuck in a routine. The querent might want more excitement. If the querent is single it can mean they are dissatisfied as singles, but are doing little to change their situation.

In a career reading the Seven of Pentacles denotes working outside. It can also mean the querent is a student.

Personality Types

The Seven of Pentacles persona is someone who has little time to enjoy their life. There is also a lack of energy to accomplish goals and dreams. These people work hard but feel like no matter how much effort they put in it will never be enough. They need to lighten up and change their attitude towards what they are trying to achieve.

They are often very close to the land and are drawn to working and spending time outside. This is someone very determined who needs to take a step back and re-evaluate. They are very patient people and plant seeds for the future. The Seven of Pentacles type people often become complacent in their jobs and end up wanting something completely different.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. Unemployment.

2. Lack of rewards.

3. Eternal student.

Meanings

When reversed the Seven of Pentacles indicates impatience, and moving forward before the time is ripe. The querent might have invested in the wrong project and are realizing the rewards that should have come will never arrive.

There is also a lack of faith in projects. There is no motivation to work. Not completing the task is the consequence. This card reversed can also indicate unemployment. It can also signal someone who loves to study, but who finds it hard stay in a job.

Personality Types

The Seven of Pentacles reversed character is someone with low self-worth, impatience and an unhealthy body image. They don’t care about their health and have problems getting motivated. They feel like they are not enough and that no one wants them. This indicates someone who is unable to enjoy the pleasures of life and they have few if any friends.

Other people end up taking the credit for their work and they often find work so unpleasant that they end up unemployed. They often lie to cover up what is really going on in their lives and how they really feel. They pretend everything is okay, when in reality it isn’t. These people enjoys studying, but find it hard to enjoy employment.

Symbolism

The seven Pentacles are connected to plants and earth symbolizing cycles, and the harvest is related to finances. The man is standing resting his hands on a farm tool, suggesting perseverance is required to see the rewards of investments. His face is contemplative, wondering if his efforts are worth the rewards.

Summary

You might be sacrificing on the material plane when the Seven of Pentacles is showing up. Seven is a spiritual number and can denote being drawn towards work that is spiritually rewarding - however, it might be low paid.

This card often shows up when people have a need to work out doors with animals or plants. The Seven of Pentacles often shows up when the querent is drawn towards working in solitude rather than in a team.
