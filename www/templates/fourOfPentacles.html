FOUR OF PENTACLES

Upright Reversed Symbolism Combinations Summary
Upright

Key Phrases

1. Status and wealth. Doing business.

2. Stubbornness. Holding on to what you know.

3. Great richness.

4. Being comfortable.

5. Recognition.

6. Financial security.

7. Being money oriented.

Meanings

The Four of Pentacles suggests the need for security and recognition in your life. This card shows financial stability and having made a solid foundation due to career advancement and rising to the top in career and business. Just make sure you don’t take a defensive stance where you are too careful to move forward to guard against losing your comfortable position.

This card shows stubbornness in the situation or in the querent. The querent needs to have control and see their money increasing. The Four of Pentacles often shows up when you have been nurturing something that later ends up turning against you in some way.

Tips for when the Four of Pentacles shows up in a reading: it speaks of the querent's relationship with money. How far are they willing to go for money? Help your querent find ways of feeling secure. When this card shows up the querent will often act as if they are poor, when they in fact have plenty.

In a love reading the Four of Pentacles speaks to money being an important factor in the relationship. Money might be the main motivation to stay in the relationship. If the querent is single the Four of Pentacles can indicate fears of giving and sharing, keeping the querent single. The querent might be only interested in a potential partner if they are very wealthy.

In a career reading the Four of Pentacles forecasts contracts involving big money. It also relate careers in well-established firms.

Personality Types

The Four of Pentacles-type person is someone who is very well-connected and is always hungry for more success and wealth. These people are very stubborn and don’t let go of their position easily.

They rarely take advice from others and therefore go through many hard life lessons, especially in their personal life. This is someone who is resisting change and is limited by their control issues.

Reversed

Key Phrases

1. A miser.

2. Hoarding.

3. Penny smart, but Pound foolish.

Meanings

When the Four of Pentacles is reversed it expresses a lack of something solid and dependable. There is nothing really reliable to hold on to. The querent might be being foolish with their money.

There is meanness with money, business problems and poor self-esteem seen in the Four of Pentacles reversed. Four of Pentacles reversed often shows up when the querent is penny smart but pound foolish, meaning that they are smart with small amounts of money, but foolish with larger amounts.

Personality Types

The Four of Pentacles reversed signals someone who is holding onto material accomplishments out of fear of failure or abandonment. They often lose their financial security due to their lack of generosity, and they become greedy or needy as a result. They believe money is the answer to all problems and measure their worth against how much is in their bank account.

This is someone who tries to impress others by pretending to be much more successful than how they really are. The Four of Pentacles reversed types are people who tend to lose track of what is really important in life.

Symbolism

The crown symbolises status. There is a coin underneath each of the man’s feet, referencing earthly possessions. He holds a coin in his arm folded in the infinity symbol, representing flow of abundance. One coin is on top of his crown signifying mastery and authority in the material world. His clothing is red with a blue trim, illustrating passion and contemplation combined.

The man is sitting on a cubic stone, suggesting stability and perfection. In the background is a city promising opportunities. The man is sitting above it on what looks like a throne, infering that the man has high social status.

Summary

The Four of Pentacles is a card that speaks of wealth on the material plane, rather than a spiritual aspect. There is the need to always have more money when the Four of Pentacles shows up. There is also a need to flaunt and impress with money. Status is of importance.

Sometimes this card shows up when the querent has a fear of being poor, and business and money matters come before friendships and intimacy. The Four of Pentacles can indicate that the querent has trust issues, especially if it shows up reversed.
